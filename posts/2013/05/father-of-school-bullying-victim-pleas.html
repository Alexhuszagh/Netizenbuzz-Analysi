Father of school bullying victim pleas to delinquents with gifts "Please don't hurt my child"

Sunday, May 12, 2013  nonentertainment, school bullying  215 comments


Article: "Please don't hurt my child"... a tearful plea

Source: SBS via Nate + Naver

A convenience store worker revealed that he saw a middle aged man come into the store with six male and three female high school students. He told them to pick whatever they wanted because he'd buy anything for them. They chose 99 items amounting to almost $400 USD in total, and the worker overheard the man pleading with the kids, "Now please don't torture my child."

SBS reporters then found the father and his daughter in question to find out more about what had happened. The students had beat up Kim, his daughter, for an hour and a half to the point where the bones in her face were crushed. She was finally discharged from the hospital last week but hasn't been able to return to school because her bulliers still remain in the neighborhood and threaten to hurt her (they were forced to transfer to different schools once the father reported what had happened).

Her parents thought that reporting the matter would help solve the issue but nothing has changed so the father eventually had to resort to "buying" favors from the students.
