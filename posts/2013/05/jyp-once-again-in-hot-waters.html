JYP once again in hot waters

Sunday, May 12, 2013  jyp entertainment  243 comments


Article: JYP evades responsibility for character products... the tyranny of a big company

Source: MBC via Naver

Last year, JYP signed a contract with a clothing company to produce 2PM's 'clothing album', a shirt that basically has a QR code you can scan to listen to their music. JYP thought that it'd sell well and agreed to produce 5,000 copies of it. Unlike what they expected, they only ended up selling 140.

JYP only paid the company the profit from what they sold, leaving the clothing company to deal with the losses of the production costs and the leftovers, which amounts to about $100,000 USD.

Other companies JYP has been working with are complaining of similar losses. A toothbrush company that produces JYP characters has seen a loss of $20,000 USD that JYP hasn't compensated for.

The companies tried to make up for the losses by selling the leftovers on their own but JYP prohibited them from doing so due to copyright, leaving the small companies to deal with the losses out of their own pockets.

JYP explained that Park Jin Young's relative was in charge of these matters and that they had no idea what was going on. JYP's marketing manager "It wasn't until the journalists came to us that we found out what was going on. We still don't know everything about the issue so we're trying to understand to the best of our ability."
