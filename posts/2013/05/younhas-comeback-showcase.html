Article: Younha "My biggest rival is Lee Hyori, I know I will get hate for this"

Source: Newsen via Nate

Asked about the comeback rush of May, she said, "I'm worried about all of them except myself."

Asked to choose one rival musician, she replied, "I think I can already predict what the article headlines will be... I know I'll get hate for 180 out of the 200... but I choose Lee Hyori senior because she's the most powerful. She's like the sun of the universe. I'm extremely nervous and am anticipating her album at the same time. Since it's her first comeback in a while, I'm curious as to what she'll be back with."
