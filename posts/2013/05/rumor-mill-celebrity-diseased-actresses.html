Unfortunately for the drama producers, her sensitive and highly emotional personality is starting to reflect on the set. One insider said, "Drama filmings are generally physically difficult. 3. Popular idol star D recently bought a large foreign SUV to copy fellow idol star E. The car is a well loved brand among top stars. Once D found out that E had recently upgraded his sedan to an SUV, D deliberated over the purchase before finally purchasing a different car under the same brand. However, some insiders said, "D doesn't match that car at all......." The reason being that the huge car doesn't fit D's small stature at all.

4. Broadcast star F has recently been receiving attention for his relentless way of speech, but it seems he wasn't born with that talent overnight. One insider who knows about F's past revealed, "I remember a few years ago he was so nervous that his hands would shake while just holding the script. We had to re-record his parts several times. It was troubling to see... He was really shy, especially moreso in front of women. Lately, however, he's become a new person." F is actually playing with veteran and more experienced MC G in the palm of his hands on variety programs. "He overcame his difficulties and now he's not scared or shy to say even stronger things on air."
