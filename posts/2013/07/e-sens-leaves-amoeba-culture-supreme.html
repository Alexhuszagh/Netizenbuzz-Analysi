E-Sens leaves Amoeba Culture + Supreme Team

Monday, July 22, 2013  e-sens, simon d, supreme team  70 comments


Article: E-Sens contract terminated, will Supreme Team disband

Source: Newsen via Nate [2]

Amoeba Culture, "On July 19th, we sent Kang Min Ho (E-Sens) a notice of termination of his exclusive contract. He is no longer an artist under Amoeba Culture."

1. [+141, -13] So disappointing that we won't be seeing a Supreme Team anymore......

2. [+140, -25] Honestly, E-Sens brought this upon himself. He probably couldn't renew his contract anyway. Why bring Simon D down like this by leaving him behind?

3. [+90, -10] What's going to happen to Supreme Team ㅠㅠ

4. [+20, -6] But Supreme Team is perfect as a duo

5. [+15, -2] Supreme Team... hul... the synergy between Simon D and E-Sens was the best... Upsetting to hear.

6. [+15, -9] Did he get into a fight with Simon D or something?

-

Simon D tweeted, "E-Sens made a decision that was the only way for him to be happy and I respect that decision. Just because we're no longer in the same team doesn't mean that our relationship of 10 years will change. We both started as solos so we'll still be able to show what we're both best at individually. I'm sorry but also grateful to everyone who loved our team's albums and waited for us."
