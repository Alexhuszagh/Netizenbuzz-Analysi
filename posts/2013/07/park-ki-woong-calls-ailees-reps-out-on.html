Park Ki Woong calls Ailee's reps out on their lie

Saturday, July 20, 2013  ailee, park ki woong  179 comments


Reader request.

-

Article: Park Ki Woong "Ailee's reps asked for me to promote her... Surprised with the response when we're the ones who helped her"

Source: Mydaily via Nate + Naver

On his Twitter, Park Ki Woong tweeted about supporting Ailee's new song. Rumors started spreading with people speculating them to be a couple. Ailee responded by saying, "Park Ki Woong and I never met. I was surprised to articles about him supporting me. As it turned out, he and my manager are close friends and he's a fan of mine. I'm grateful for his support."

After the article about Ailee claiming that she doesn't know Park Ki Woong went out, Park Ki Woong tweeted, "Ailee's side asked us to promote her new song acting like I was close to her. As someone who helped her out, I was really surprised with what the article said yesterday. Ailee called me afterwards and we talked it out. I really do support her, though! I like U&I."
