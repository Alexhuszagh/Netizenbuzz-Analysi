Wednesday, July 10, 2013  nonentertainment  389 comments

Article: Teen murders and cuts up another teen 'shock'

Source: No Cut News via Nate [2] [3] [4]

A 19 year old male teen was arrested for murdering a 17 year old female. Police received a report from her parents on the 9th saying they could not reach contact with her. Police narrowed the suspects down to a few until the teen turned himself in on the 10th.

According to investigations, he had called her out to a motel with the intent of raping her. Once she began fighting back, he choked her to death.

****gory details following***

He confessed that he decided to cut up her body and leave the motel once he was sure he killed her. He went to a nearby convenience store to buy some knives and came back only to find that they weren't sharp enough to cut through the body. Desperate, he began cutting pieces of her skin off and flushing it down the toilet.

Her face was severely mutilated to the point where the majority of the skin was gone and the bones were broken and separated into 20 different pieces. Police suspect that he went through three knives to achieve this.

He then left the motel in the early morning to purchase plastic bags and checked out with the body parts after a total of 16 hours. He grabbed a cab and went home and left the bags inside a container in front of his home.
