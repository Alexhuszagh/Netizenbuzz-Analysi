'Monstar' drama staff accused of sexually assaulting three extras

Monday, July 01, 2013  monstar, sexual assault, tv  94 comments


Article: [Exclusive] Drama extras sexually assaulted once again...

Source: Segye Ilbo via Nate

A drama manager of a cable drama is being accused of sexually assaulting three drama extras (two minors).  A (21) filed the report and claimed that he 'B' (33) called her out and groped her chest and behind as well as ask her toget on all fours in her school uniform skirt while he stood behind and watched.

He also allegedly asked two other high school extras to kiss him on the lips and groped them as well as punishment for catching them smoking. A claimed that the drama producer and her agency did not do anything about it, and A quit the drama herself.

In an interview, B denied all claims, saying, "I only reprimanded the extras in question for smoking. I wiped their butt off once because there was dust on it. I can't help it if they felt uncomfortable with that. It's absolutely not true that I groped their chest and butt. Everything will be revealed through police investigations."

The controversy is growing because this comes just after in 2009, when a woman in her thirties commit suicide after being sexually assaulted by a drama company staff when she was working as an extra.
