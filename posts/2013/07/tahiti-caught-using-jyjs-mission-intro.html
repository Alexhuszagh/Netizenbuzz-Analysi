TAHITI caught using JYJ's "Mission" intro without permission

Wednesday, July 10, 2013  jyj, mission, tahiti  180 comments


Article: Copy the 'Intro'? Girl group Tahiti under controversy for using JYJ song without permission

Source: No Cut News via Naver

Tahiti used JYJ's "Mission" intro in one of their performances at a college festival and said in an interview with the MC after, "This intro is usually only specially used for our international performances but we're using it for this college performance today."

Tahiti used the same intro at performance in Cambodia back in March as well. Fans alerted the matter to Dream Star Entertainment. Reps apologized and explained that the intro was originally used by suggestion of an international concert rep and their manager didn't think to check before using it again for other performances.

"We should've just used it once and left it at that but we didn't know so we used it at Cambodia and at the college festival. Had we known that the song belonged to JYJ, we would not have used it."
