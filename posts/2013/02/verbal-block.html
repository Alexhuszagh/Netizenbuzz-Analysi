Tuesday, February 19, 2013  verbal jint  126 comments


I sometimes get requests for indie/underground artists and the only underground artist consistently in the rankings is Verbal Jint.

He has a bit of a negative image with netizens, though, thanks to a few overzealous fans. If you follow him on Twitter, you're probably familiar with the fact that he gets bombarded with "fans" spamming just to get a mention or repeating questions he already answered about his albums/concerts (as with any other celebrity). Verbal Jint deals with them by just blocking them off of his Twitter. Fair enough, right?
