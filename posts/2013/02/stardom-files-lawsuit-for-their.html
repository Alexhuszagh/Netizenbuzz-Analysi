Monday, February 25, 2013  block b, brand new music, stardom entertainment  63 comments


Article: Stardom files an injunction for de facto power

Source: Newsen via Nate

Stardom has filed a lawsuit against former CEO 'Kim' (TN: I'm assuming Rhymer) and manager 'Lee', as well as the Block B members as witnesses. Stardom claims that Kim and the other staff members tricked the company and the Block B members while they were still under the company by embezzling money made from events and purposely leaving out income receipts/records.

Stardom also claims that Kim and three others have been talking to broadcast companies and related associates that they have a plan underway to "take Block B out of the company". They claim that they used the members' young ages/naivety to trick them and cause discord within the company.

    the artists/ their parents don't understand their contracts or the industry well enough.
