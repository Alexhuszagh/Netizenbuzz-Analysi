Adult TV show 'Love and War 2' to bring in an all idol cast

Wednesday, February 13, 2013  love and war 2, tv  71 comments


Article: Idol version of 'Love and War 2' to be released... March broadcast

Source: Star News via Naver

'Love and War' is a show that brings in couples to share whatever their problem is whether it be about their marriage (affairs, divorce) or family before a panel of professionals. The show will reenact the scenario before experts give their advice and a conclusion is drawn.

For its second season, the show has decided to use idols to act out the scenarios in place of their usual cast of actors.

"Idols We will be looking for idols who are able to act out the emotions required of the scenarios. Depending on who we're able to cast, the scenarios that we pick out will be different. Viewers will be able to see idols act out the problems between newlyweds in their 20s and 30s and other conflicts that arise within families. We're still in the process of casting, which is proving to be a bit difficult due to the heavy image of the show."
