'89 line idols are on the rise

Wednesday, February 13, 2013  89 line  74 comments



Article: Music industry's '89 line', snake zodiac idols are on the rise

Source: Asia Economy via Naver

Filler article talking about how the '89 line idols are on the rise, especially with most being members of groups 3-4 years into their debut. Listed idols are six members from SNSD, Sunye, Yeeun, Ailee, Hyomin, Hyosung, Yewon for the girls.

For the guys, Daesung, Onew, Jo Kwon, Wooyoung, Yonghwa, Shin Yong Jae, all of B2ST minus maknae (some are early '90), and ZE:A's Junyoung, Heechul, and Taeheon.

The article reasons that the reason there are so many '89 line idols is because they grew up watching the first generation idol groups hit jackpot (HOT, Sechs Kies, SES, Finkl, Shinhwa, GOD, BoA) when they were in elementary/junior high school. The article ends with their zodiac fortunes for the year lol..
