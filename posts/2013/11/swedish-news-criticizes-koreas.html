Swedish news criticizes Korea's education system

Saturday, November 02, 2013  nonentertainment  954 comments


Article: "Education should not be done like the Koreans do it" <Swedish News>

Source: Yonhap News via Nate

A Swedish documentary called the 'Miracle of Korean Education' talked about how Korean students are ranked at the highest in the world and credited it to their 17-hour study habits and expensive after school academies along with societal + parental pressure. Students barely sleep four hours a night as a result.

"Korea's education system revolves around textbooks and testing. Students rarely have an opportunity for group or autonomous activities. Students are essentially oppressed and the pressure put on these teens is too great. Korea needs to be concerned about the longterm effects this will have on them. Sweden needs to improve our own education system but not through Korea's method of having students study over 60 hours a week."
