How much longer can T-ara ignore the public?

Thursday, April 11, 2013  core contents media, t-ara  250 comments



Article: T-ara unit, random mysterious concept with 'forced' promotions

Source: TV Daily via Nate

tl;dr: Journalist criticizes CCM/T-ara for ignoring the public and continuing to "force their promotions" onto them.

"T-ara doesn't have any of the conditions in which a mysterious marketing tactic would work. Despite the fact that they disturbed society with a bullying scandal last year, they forced their 'Sexy Love' promotions, which only made them grow further apart with the public. Going forward with a mysterious marketing tactic now can only be seen as random.  
Unlike Kang Ho Dong and Kim Gura, other figures who also disturbed society, T-ara ignored the criticisms of the public and continued to force their way through promotions. After 'Sexy Love', the group not only appeared at year-end events, but also continued their individual member activities. They did all this ignoring the fact that the audience and the public grew quiet whenever it was T-ara's turn for the stage.  
What should be questioned now is whether T-ara can continue to promote while ignoring the public. The public's dislike for the group is still apparent so for T-ara to still be able to appear on broadcast shows that the music industry and the broadcast industry are inseparable.  
Core Contents Media CEO Kim Kwang Soo has been in the management industry for a while now and thus knows plenty of connections with broadcast PDs, which explains how T-ara was still able to be 'tossed in' music and variety shows even after their bullying scandal. Of course, they were never given full coverage roles.  
The topic of curiosity now is how much T-ara will expose themselves with their unit promotions. Core Contents Media is already attacking the public by unleashing their pollution onto them in the form of unit promotions."
