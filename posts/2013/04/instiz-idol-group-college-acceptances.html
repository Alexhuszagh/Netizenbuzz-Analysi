[Instiz] Idol group college acceptances without special treatment

Saturday, April 13, 2013  btob, instiz  106 comments

BTOB: Five were accepted into college before their debut (Sungjae is still a high school student) 


Eunkwang: Dongshin University / Applied Music major


Minhyuk: Danguk University / Musical major (major is famous for its difficulty to get into, Minhyuk got in for getting excellent marks his freshman year of high school) Also was accepted to Myungji University's musical major.


Changsub: Howon university / Applied Music major (Howon's applied music major is one of the top three best in Korea. Changsub applied twice and tested with 2,800 students competing for five seats in the major and earned the #1 grade)


Hyunshik: Howon University / Applied Music major


Peniel: Hanyang University / International Affairs major

-

And these next two were in another thread for "male idols who are often mistaken for receiving special treatment but got into college on their own merit"

SHINee Minho: Konkuk University / Theater/Film major

B2ST Dongwoon: Konkuk University / Film major (held himself back a year and took the college entrance exams twice to get in)
