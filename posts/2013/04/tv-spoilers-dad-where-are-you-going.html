I'm sorry I put off this request for so long. Feel free to let me know if there's a specific kid or part of an episode you want to see reactions to. Article: 'Dad!' continues its popularity, but this is the time where decisions are most important

 Source: OSEN via Nate

"MBC's 'Dad! Where are you going?' is continuing its streak of popularity. Three months since its first episode, the show's viewer ratings remain high. Although it's recently suffered a drop from its highest record, the show remains in the 10% area, and the kids are still as popular as ever. Not only have they taken up various CFs and advertisements, but news regarding the children in the media receive ecstatic responses.
However, it's important to be careful when you're at the height of your popularity. There are some opinions regarding the drop in viewer ratings of the show 'beginning to lose its flavor'. Many are also fearful of the children turning into products of the entertainment industry when they have not yet matured...."

1. [+310, -18] None of us are expecting variety antics from the five children. We watch this show because it heals your heart and mind, much more so than 'Healing Camp'. Please just show us the children comfortably and as is! I trust that the producers won't force anything onto the children for the sake of the show! ^^

2. [+232, -83] It won't be good for the children for them to be on the show for so long, and of course, viewers are bound to get bored some day... I'd like to suggest that the show switches out members every three months, although it'd be difficult to see the children go...

3. [+53, -4] I think it'd be best to end the show when it's the most loved by the viewers. The longer the show goes on, the more haters it will garner, and I'm just afraid of the children getting hurt in the end.

4. [+48, -1] I think a change will be good too. Not necessarily changing the members but something different from going grocery shopping and cooking dinner. Since it's spring, maybe spring-related activities... or going on vacation with their other siblings.

5. [+35 -1] I at first wanted the show to go on for many more seasons but after seeing stupid people start comparing the children... I don't even want to see what will happen once new children are brought onto the show and how people will compare them to the old cast. Nobody ever thought the show would hit daebak like this anyway. Just end the show around May... There are already hateful replies coming out here and there. I don't want the children to be exposed to this so early on...

 Article: 'Dad!' Yoon Hoo "There are no bad people here" 

The episode featured a hidden camera where the kids discover a few coins on the side of the road. Their dads asked them to step on it and protect it while they go off somewhere and they have another dad come in and try to take the coin from them. Hoo refused to give up the coin to Kim Sung Joo saying, "It's not mine, we have to find the owners. My dad went to find the owner of the coins."
After his dad came back and said he couldn't find the owner, Hoo put the coin back down. The fathers then tried to convince him to take the coin so that he can buy toys with it but Hoo refused and said, "If the real owner sees the coin, he can take it. There are no bad people here. Only nice people."
