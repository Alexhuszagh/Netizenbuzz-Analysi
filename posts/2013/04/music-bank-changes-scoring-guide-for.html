'Music Bank' changes scoring guide for broadcast points

Saturday, April 20, 2013  music bank, psy  127 comments


Article: 'Music Bank' changes their scoring guide... Netizens complain "Their intentions are obvious"

Source: OSEN via Naver

Starting from the 19th, Music Bank changed the way broadcast points are scored, which make up 20% of a singer's final score. Before, every time a song was played on any program, including news, it counted as 1 point. They changed it so that news programs are excluded and so that songs can only count once even if they are played several times on the same show.
