Monday, April 08, 2013  hello, nonentertainment, wtf  59 comments



Article: 'Hello' Number girl "My name is Lee 0, I can't even change my name"

Source: Newsen via Nate

"My name is '0'. My last name is Lee and my first name is '0'. People never know how to read my name so they'll call me 'Lee Gong', 'Lee Oh', 'Lee Zero', or 'Lee Circle'. It's a name my father thought up. I can't even change it because he's stubborn and adamant that I keep it. 
No electronic system recognizes my name. I can't even join internet websites. People always ask me why I stopped writing my name and ask me to finish writing it out properly 
My father named me like this because he wanted to give me a unique name, a one of a kind in the world. It took me a week to get my driver's license."
