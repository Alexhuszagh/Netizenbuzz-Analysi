College students surveyed on North Korea

Monday, April 15, 2013  nonentertainment, north korea  62 comments



Article: 32% of college students "I will fight in the case of war"... 12% to run away overseas

Source: No Cut News via Nate

457 college students were surveyed. The results were as follows:
- 84% A war will not happen
- 15.8% We are on the threshold of war
- 53.2% I will not fight in the war but I will remain in Korea
- 32.6% I will fight/participate in the war
- 12.3% I will run away overseas
- 28.4% North Korea is South Korea's enemy
- 35.9% North Korea needs cooperation with South Korea
- 21.4% North Korea and South Korea are one Korean race
- 63% North and South Korea must be reunified
