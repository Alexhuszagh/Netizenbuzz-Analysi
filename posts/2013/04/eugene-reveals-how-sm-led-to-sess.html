Eugene reveals how SM led to SES's disbandment

Thursday, April 25, 2013  eugene, ses, sm entertainment  452 comments


Article: 'Knee-Drop Guru' Eugene, "The reason SES disbanded? SM wanted to renew contracts with individual members (not the entire group)"

Source: Mydaily via Nate

KHD: "Did you decide to disband SES over money?"

Eugene: "Bada, Shoo, and I wanted to renew our contracts as SES but SM did not want to renew us as a group but as individual members. Our first contracts were individual as well so I think they wanted to just continue it that way. Eventually, we weren't able to reach a compromise so the three of us decided to disband while we were at the height of our popularity.

Groups these days can have members doing individual activities while still remaining as a group, but such a concept didn't exist back then. People thought you were betraying your group if you did something on your own so we had to stay together. That's why we chose to disband. If the current system existed back then, we would've been able to remain together..."
