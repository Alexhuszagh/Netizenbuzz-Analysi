Journalists prove they're big babies :'(

Tuesday, April 30, 2013  ryu hyun kyung  70 comments


Article: Media outlets angered over an actress who refused an interview, revenge taken through articles

Source: Media Today via Naver

Actress Ryu Hyun Kyung attended a press con type event for her upcoming movie where she was asked about her dating rumors with Jang Giha. She denied the rumors and asked that journalists stay on topic and refrain from asking further questions about it.

Journalists (TV Report in particular), however, wrote up several articles sensationalizing the rumors again so RHK's reps asked that the articles all be deleted.

RHK then said that she felt uncomfortable being interviewed by the same TV Report journalist who wrote the malicious articles about her and asked TV Report to send another journalist for her next interview. They complied but backed out at the last minute saying that the new journalist had another schedule to attend to so they would be sending the original journalist. RHK never actually refused the interview with the journalist in question; her company just expressed that she felt uncomfortable working with him again.

Pissed off, TV Report started releasing false articles claiming that RHK was boycotting all interviews. They then went further by bringing up her older controversies and putting them back in the spotlight, as well as making up controversies for her attitude on her recent guesting on 'Running Man'.

Other outlets like Media Day, Asia Economy, Sports Seoul, News 1, and Chosun Ilbo joined the hate train for no reason and released their own articles under malicious titles like:

""This isn't the first time Ryu Hyun Kyung has boycotted journalists"

"Ryu Hyun Kyung on the chopping block for hateful statement 'Why would I date an idiot like Jang Giha?'"

"Ryu Hyun Kyung's boycott vs Lee Kyung Kyu's hard work promotion, contrasting work ethics"

"'Asshi, isshi' 'Running Man' Can Ryu Hyun Kyung really say these things on air?"

"Ryu Hyun Kyung's boycott causes a stir, who does she think she is?"

"Ryu Hyun Kyung's solitary boycott, 'I won't do it if I don't want to'"

"Ryu Hyun Kyung boycotts the journalist who released a dating rumor article despite explanation"

"Ryu Hyun Kyung on the chopping block for boycott controversy... why?"

Other journalists are divided on what's going on. One journalist feels that RHK's attitude in thinking that she can boycott journalists and interviews just because they wrote a bad article about her isn't right. Another journalist feels that TV Report is acting purely out of emotions and that it isn't right for them to be using the media like this to get their revenge on her.
