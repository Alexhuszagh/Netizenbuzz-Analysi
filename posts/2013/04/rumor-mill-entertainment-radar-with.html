Thursday, April 25, 2013  rumor mill  347 comments


To clear things up because I get asked this all the time - most rumor mills don't have netizen guesses because netizens either don't know or never commented. THIS article in particular does because it was up in the rankings and had best replies with guesses, etc, so they'll be included at the end of each item.

-

1. Popular idol group member C is undeniably the hottest star of the music industry. Netizens have noticed something suspicious going on between C and popular girl group member D, but recent rumors say that he also might be developing feelings for another popular idol group member E. C and D were a trending topic online after accounts of people spotting them at a famous club together in Seoul at the end of the year last night began making its round online. Recently, however, the direction of the rumors have changed, as netizens now suspect C to have something going on with E. C and E are both known for not following other celebrities on SNS, but not only do they follow each other, C also likes every single one of E's pictures (TN: the SNS mentioned here is Instagram, the topic has come up often on community boards of C liking a bunch of E's pictures in a row). While one side argues that C also follows plenty of other celebrities and likes plenty of other pictures, one industry insider said, "We don't know for sure if C ever dated D or is dating E but I guess this just proves what an amazing star he is considering all of the rumors he gets into with popular female stars."

C = G-Dragon
D = Lizzy
E = Krystal

2. Celebrities F and G who always denied being in a relationship whenever they were swept under rumors together are said to be actually dating. The two became lovers after starring in the same drama and have gone on dates either with other celebrity friends or in their car. When news of their relationship got out, both companies caused a fit saying that it wasn't possible, but it seems that even their agencies weren't aware of what has been going on. One insider said, "G has always been known for her innocent image so it's possible that people find it hard to imagine her in a relationship. G's CEO is under her power so it's completely possible."

F = Yonghwa
G = Park Shin Hye
