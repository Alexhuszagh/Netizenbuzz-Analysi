Music Bank's viewer ratings continue to plummet

Saturday, April 06, 2013  busker busker, music bank  90 comments



Article: 'Music Bank' falls deeper endlessly, there seems to be no exit

 Source: OSEN via Naver

Article that talks about how Music Bank has been consistently recording lower and lower viewer ratings (2.2% this week). Journalist suggests that Music Bank stop focusing on catering to companies and petty company rivalry and start focusing on what the viewers want. An example used is Busker Busker, who's never performed on Music Bank since their debut because they are from rival program Mnet. Their ranking and numbers clearly show that they are a viewer's favorite and the journalist suggests that Music Bank focus on presenting good music and performances instead of unfair ranking systems and petty rivalry.

1. [+25, -3] Right, Busker Busker should perform on Music Bank

2. [+16, -1] The article is right. All three music shows need to reflect over this. 90% of the performers are idols so aside from the loyal teen fans, viewers have pretty much turned on the shows. Give real artists a chance so that we the viewers can listen to some music that's actually worth calling music.

3. [+11, -1] Who would watch a show that puts up talentless idols on stage?

4. [+9, -0] I stopped watching the show because I got tired of Lee Jang Woo. The show is basically a dance battle between idols nobody knows.

5. [+9, -0] Journalist, would YOU want to watch a program like this?

6. [+9, -0] Agree with the comments, there needs to be a more variety of performances. It seems they just throw up anyone on stage as long as they're an idol.

7. [+9, -1] How can they expect anyone to watch a show when they're unfairly ranking songs they picked out of a trash can. On top of that, the real popular song never receives anything. All you need to do is come out on TV often and buy back your albums for a #1 trophy~
