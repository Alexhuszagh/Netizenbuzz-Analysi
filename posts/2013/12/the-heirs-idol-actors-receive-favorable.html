Friday, December 13, 2013  krystal, minhyuk, park hyung shik, the heirs  120 comments

3. [+633, -46] Hyungshik really pulled off the Myungsoo role well

7. [+141, -7] Krystal's acting is still a bit sitcom style but she does well with grouchy/attitude dependent characters. Stupid~

8. [+135, -7] Krystal was awkward at first but improved a lot with the chemistry development with Kang Min Hyuk. Kang Min Hyuk did well as well as Park Hyungshik Kim Woo Bin, though... I really became his fan after this.

9. [+146, -18] I think they each had just the right amount of screentime to earn a good review. They wouldn't have done so well in lead roles.
