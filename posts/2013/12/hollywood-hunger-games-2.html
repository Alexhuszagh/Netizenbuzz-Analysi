Thursday, December 05, 2013  hollywood, jennifer lawrence, the hunger games 2  534 comments


Article: Box office daebak 'Hunger Games 2', why is it receiving a lukewarm response in Korea?

Source: Newsen via Nate

1. I personally loved the novel and found the second movie more enjoyable than the first.

6. [+17, -7] Well, the first movie didn't do well in Korea so of course the second won't do well (I blame the lack of marketing, there are a ton of people who have never even heard of the title).. and unlike the Harry Potter series where you can jump into any of the movies and follow along, you're lost for 30 minutes in The Hunger Games 2 if you never saw the first one.
