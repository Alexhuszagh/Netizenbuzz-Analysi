Opinions divided over INFINITE's managers shouting at rowdy international fans at the airport

Wednesday, December 04, 2013  infinite  287 comments


Article: "Yah, get out!" INFINITE's managers under controversy for shouting informal Korean... fancam opinions divided

Source: Kuki News via Nate

Fancams show INFINITE's managers shouting at rowdy international fans at the airport in a bit of an aggressive/rough manner like "Get out", "I said get out!", "move". Netizens were divided in opinion, with some stating that the managers were giving a bad image to the group and Korea by shouting like that and others stating that the managers probably had no choice but to shout like that for INFINITE's safety.

Apparently, anti-Korean Japanese blogs are also using the fancams as examples of ill Korean behavior, saying, "This is the true image of Koreans. They'll smile brightly at a concert they're paid for but yell at you if you follow them around at the airport because you like them. Hallyu? What a joke."
