30 female top stars under suspicion for prostitution

Thursday, December 12, 2013  wtf  301 comments


Background info on this: Last night, a bunch of articles came out about 30 female top stars being involved in prostitution. Simultaneously, articles about another issue (the president pushing for the privatization of railroads, which would hike up the prices of train tickets astronomically) were mysteriously taken down.

Some netizens began to notice this so when people were searching for the names of the alleged female stars, they started planting the initials 'M Y H' in the comments, saying she was one of the stars. Then netizens went further and claimed it was a star named Min Young Hwa, which led to the name being the most searched keyword on portal sites.

The sneaky part about it is that Min Young Hwa is not the name of an actual star, but rather the word for 'privatization', so netizens were able to redirect the attention from the scandal back to the privatization issue. Whether it's really a conspiracy.. who knows, but it was a cool move on the netizens' part since it brought attention to a more important issue anyway.
