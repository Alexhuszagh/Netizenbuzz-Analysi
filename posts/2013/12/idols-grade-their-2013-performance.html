Idols grade their 2013 performance

Tuesday, December 24, 2013    127 comments



Article: [The replies of 76 singers] "What would I grade myself this year?"

Source: MBN via Naver

- miss A: Getting our performance style known through 'Hush' [★★★★☆] "During our 'Hush' promotions, we received a lot of praise on our performance, which allowed us to promote more actively."

- K. Will: Get a good review on songs other than the title track in my album [★★★★☆] "I think I succeeded somewhat in receiving a good review on my other songs. What I think I did well this year is how hard I worked."

- SISTAR: [★★★★★] "It was a year where we received the most love. Thank you for that love."

- INFINITE: [★★★★☆] "We think we successfully completed our broadcast, album, and world tour promotions."

- Ailee: Release great music [★★★☆☆] "I think I achieved my goal. I released great music for the public. My most memorable event this year was attending year-end ceremonies."

- B2ST: [★★★☆☆] "We think we upgraded our group's image through our 'Shadow' promotions. It was a memory to cherish with our fans."

- MIB: To have people think of MIB when they hear hip hop group [★★☆☆☆] "We released three albums and promoted actively, but we think we need to work harder."

- EXO: Get our existence known [★★★★☆] "It was the best year where we achieved everything we dreamed of. THank you for your love."

- A Pink: Promote actively after our long hiatus [★★★☆☆] "We made great memories with our fans on stage and promoted actively as well, but time flew by a lot faster than we thought."

- VIXX: Buy our fans chicken after winning #1 [★★★★★] "We ran without rest and were paid with the best present of the year. We were glad to buy our fans chicken after winning #1 on a music program."

- f(x): [★★★★☆] "It's disappointing that we couldn't promote our music for a longer time but we were glad to get that much closer with our fans through an amazing album."

- 4minute: Capture the public with 'What's Your Name' [★★★★☆] "We think What's Your Name received a lot of love from the public, which we're grateful for. The promotions allowed us to communicate with our fans. Although we are still lacking, we'll work hard."

- Nine Muses: Promote actively without rest [★★★★☆] ""From 'Gun' to 'Glue', we promoted consistently all year long. We want to use this year as a foundation to work harder next year. It was a good decision for us to fight with our CEO to able to release a full album (laughter)."

- Girl's Day: Win #1 on a music program [★★★☆☆] "We won #1 on a music program. Time has flown by fast with all that we've done. It was a precious year for us."
