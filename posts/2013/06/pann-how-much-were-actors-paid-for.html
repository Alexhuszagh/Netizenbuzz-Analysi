And yeah, Iris and The Legend were high-budget productions, so investors were willing to cough up the big bucks.

Because most traditional sageuks are quality productions widely respected by most Koreans, most top stars jump at the chance to be in one. Endorsements and accolades down the line will more than make up for the lower paid.

    It's too bad that great veteran actors like The Chaser's Son Hyun Joo and Deep-Rooted Tree's Han Suk Gyu's are paid so much less than acting duds like Kim Tae Hee, Song Seung Hun and Han Ga In.
