Lee Joon's statement on money management earns respect

Thursday, June 13, 2013  lee joon, mblaq  166 comments


Article: Lee Joon's respectable statement "You're deluding yourself if you think that your idol income will last forever"

Source: TV Daily via Daum

Lee Joon: "We make more money than most people our age, and people tend to delude themselves into thinking that we'll be making this kind of money forever. Your career's over the minute you think that, though.

Until now, my only dream has been to make money. I was able to help out when my aunt fell sick. She recovered when I went to go see her. I'm proud of myself for spending my money in this way while others are buying foreign cars. My goal for now is for everyone around me to be happy."
