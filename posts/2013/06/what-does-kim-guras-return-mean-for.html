What does Kim Gura's return mean for Kyuhyun on 'Radio Star'?

Monday, June 03, 2013  kim gura, kyuhyun, radio star, tv, variety  59 comments



Article: With Kim Gura's return, how will he work with Kyuhyun in 'Radio Star'?

Source: Mediaus via Nate

1. [+122, -22] Before he left the show, I felt that he had good teamwork with Kyuhyun. I think Yoo Se Yoon filled in his absence very naturally.

2. [+104, -9] Kim Gura's already worked with Kyuhyun before, I don't know why anyone would be worried with how they'll mesh. Kim Gura was the one that suggested Kyuhyun after seeing his variety shows on Friend Note. Everyone was thinking Eunhyuk to take Heechul's place but Kim Gura was the only one who chose Kyuhyun.

3. [+88, -6] Kim Gura suggested Kyuhyun anyway. All Kyuhyun has to do is shed his 'poison words' image and go back to his original position of backing up what the others say.

4. [+25, -1] It's time for Kyuhyun and Kim Gura to fight like Kim Gura did with Shin Jung Hwan

5. [+22, -1] I think Kim Gura worked better with Kyuhyun than Heechul. Kim Gura makes it really obvious when he's around or working with people he doesn't particularly like, and you could really tell that he was uncomfortable with Heechul. On the contrary, you could tell that Kim Gura paid extra attention to Kyuhyun, especially since he's the one that got him on the show.

6. [+15, -3] I'll be honest, Kyuhyun is fu*king hilarious At first, I was wondering what they were doing putting a kid like that on the show but the more you watch him, the more his poisonous words are hilarious

7. [+15, -1] I think they're going to use Heechul, just like how Infinity Challenge made fun of Gil as the 'Haha spare'. That'll raise Kyuhyun's variety level.

8. [+14, -2] Kyuhyun was like a cat in a strange garret but now I feel like the show would be so empty without him. I'm looking forward to how this will play out.

9. [+14, -3] Kyuhyun's actually quite hilarious though I think the funniest out of the MCs on the show right now. Kim Gura's return will mean that he'll be going the hardest with his poisonous words so Kyuhyun can stick to the weaker ones They'll work well together.

10. [+14, -1] It's funny to me how Kyuhyun's gaze looks so sparkly and innocent but he's not afraid to go hard with his words
