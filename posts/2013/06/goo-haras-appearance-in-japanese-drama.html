Article: [Exclusive] Goo Hara starring in a popular Japanese drama brings out anti-Korean sentiment

Source: OSEN via Nate + Naver

Goo Hara starred in Fukuyama Masaharu's drama 'Galileo' and called it an honor to be able to work in such a popular drama. Japanese netizens started accusing Fuji TV of using Hallyu to raise their viewer ratings, commenting, "They're forcing Hallyu for the viewer ratings", "Fuji TV is going strong for the popularity", and "If the show gets high viewer ratings, the Korean Japanese will feel more powerful."

A TV magazine editor wrote, "'Galileo' has been achieving over 20% in viewer ratings so there's no point in adding Goo Hara to the line-up because the drama's already popular and Hallyu is on the downfall. This can only be seen as Fuji TV forcing themselves to support the Hallyu wave."

Another Hallyu magazine journalist claimed, "It's not Fuji TV but rather Fukuyama's agency CEO. He is the one who brought over Korean movies like 'Shiri' and 'JSA' and made them hits in Japan. He is the leader of the Hallyu wave. I believe that the CEO is now trying to break Korean musicals into the Japanese market and is thus focusing on the Hallyu wave even more now."
