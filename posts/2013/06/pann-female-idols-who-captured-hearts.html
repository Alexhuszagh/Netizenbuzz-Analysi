[Pann] Female idols who captured the hearts of men by year

Sunday, June 02, 2013  pann  87 comments

 

2007~2008 Sohee

 

With the 'Tell Me' syndrome, she began being called 'Mandoo Sohee' and achieved unbelievable popularity

 

2009 Taeyeon

 

The 'Gee' syndrome hit 9 weeks of consecutive wins on Music Bank. Her cute looks and personality captured the hearts of men everywhere. 

 

2010 Sulli

 

Starting from 2009 through 2010, she shot to fame with NuABO and was named the ideal woman of most men. Earned nicknames like Sulovely, Sulpretty, etc. 

 

2011 IU

 

No need for explanations. Her 'Good Day' syndrome catapulted her as the nation's little sister and earned public recognition/popularity. 

 

2012~2013 Suzy

CF Queen and the nation's first love. Dramas have been a hit and she's since garnered a fanbase of both men and women.
