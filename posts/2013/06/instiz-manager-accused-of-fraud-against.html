[Instiz] Manager accused of fraud against the late Park Yong Ha revealed to be VIXX's current manager

Thursday, June 06, 2013  jellyfish entertainment, park yong ha, vixx  96 comments

Summary: The late Park Yong Ha's former manager is VIXX's current manager. This is problematic because she was accused for trying to steal Park Yong Ha's money a week after his death.

Instiz: Despite suspicions of fraud and theft, Lee is currently a manager for VIXX



Manager Lee was extremely close with Park Yong Ha, to the point where she read his funeral address at his funeral. Although she herself had fainted upon hearing the news of his sudden death, she acted as a pillar to his friends and family at his funeral, patting the backs of male celebrities who broke out into tears and consoling the actresses who called out a name who could never answer again.

After delivering her funeral address, she collapsed to the floor, receiving the consolation of So JI Sub and Ryu Shi Won.

-

The problem occurred one week after. Not even a week after Park Yong Ha took his own life, the same manager was accused of trying to transfer $200,000 USD from Park Yong Ha's account into her own by illegally using his stamp/signature. She also took $26,000 worth of Park Yong Ha merchandise (albums, photobooks) from the company.

When netizens expressed their rage against what had happened, the manager gave an interview saying that she had every right to use that money and that she had taken the photobooks/albums to preserve his memory.

-


It was most recently found out that despite these charges, she's still working in the industry as VIXX's manager. Once netizens caught wind of the news, JellyFish Entertainment released a statement saying that she was never taken to court and that she's a hard worker and that they'll 'restrict' her, but she's still seen working as VIXX's manager.
