[WTF] Woman brags about water bottle delivery

Saturday, August 31, 2013  nonentertainment, wtf  204 comments


Article: "Please deliver 168 water bottles" controversy...

Source: Money Today via Nate

A woman uploaded that picture along with a post on a community board bragging about how she made an old delivery man deliver 168 water bottles in one day.

"I was at work when the delivery man called me at 8 a.m. shocked at the delivery load, asking how he was supposed to deliver this all and asking me to save him.

I live on the 4th floor but the distance might as well make it the 5th floor. I get out of breath just walking up the stairs on my own. The delivery man told me that he won't be able to work if he delivers all this and that he will be sick in bed for days after. He asked if he could deliver a pack a day, a weight he could manage carrying around. But of course, I'm not one to agree to that. I demanded that he deliver them all today no matter what.

That man probably fainted after delivering all of this. I did this to him before and he always asked what kind of house I was running to need all of this."

Once the post became controversial online, the woman's younger sister wrote a new post saying that her sister exaggerated what really happened because she just wanted to brag about winning all those water bottles through a contest. "There was an event where you could order water bottles at half price so my sister ordered 10 packs for same day delivery. The delivery man called and asked if he could deliver the packs once a day instead of all at once but she said no because she's not home every day and asked for them to all be delivered to her front door. She then asked our father to help the delivery man carry them up the stairs."
