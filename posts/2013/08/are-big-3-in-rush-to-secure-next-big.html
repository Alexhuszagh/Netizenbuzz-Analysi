Are the 'Big 3' in a rush to secure the next big thing?

Tuesday, August 20, 2013  jyp entertainment, sm entertainment, yg entertainment  948 comments


Reader request.

-

Article: [Enterbiz] SM-YG-JYP, change the main faces! What's rushing them?

Source: Sports Chosun via Nate

Article talks about how insiders have been noticing that SM, YG, and JYP seem to be in a rush for change and finding the next new thing to represent their company for the next decade.

The article says that SNSD, TVXQ, Super Junior (for SM), Big Bang and 2NE1 (for YG), and 2PM and Suzy (for JYP) are all still doing well but the problem is that they're so successful that nobody really anticipates anything more from them anymore. Surprising news from them would be if they DIDN'T do well, putting the three companies in a rush to find fresh new talent to continue the trend.

The article thinks that SM is investing in f(x) and EXO, YG in their new boy and girl group debuts, and JYP in Sunmi's solo.
