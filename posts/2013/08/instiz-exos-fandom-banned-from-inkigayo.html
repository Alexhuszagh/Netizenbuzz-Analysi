[Instiz] EXO's fandom banned from Inkigayo

Sunday, August 04, 2013  exo, instiz, telzone  1425 comments


Notice basically says that EXO fans will no longer be allowed into the Inkigayo recording hall, as Inkigayo alerted fandoms that they will no longer be distributing entrance tickets specifically to the EXO fandom because they've failed to follow staff orders and have broken too many safety rules (running into the street to chase the members, using cheer items in dangerous ways that put others at risk, police have also been receiving reports, etc).

Source: Instiz

"I'm serious at a loss as to how much more the rest of us have to apologize on behalf of fanclub members like that. I want EXO to go on a long two year hiatus so that they'll leave the fandom. What's the point of waiting for EXO for a year if we can't even go see them?"

"They should realize that the image of fans = image of the artist."

"I saw with my own eyes just how bad the EXO fandom can be at the Busan festival today. They ran to the front and shoved everyone once people were being admitted, and other fans were on the floor being stepped on. I even saw a few try to sneak in through holes in the fences."

"This is getting really serious, isn't it..."

"I think at this point, if the fandom doesn't fix itself, it won't only be the fandom that isn't allowed at broadcast halls but EXO as well."
