Too many idols on too many variety shows

Sunday, August 18, 2013  exo, lee joon, tv, variety  561 comments




Article: Having idol guests on variety shows are no longer a cure all solution

Source: Mediaus via Nate

Editorial that talks about how variety shows are throwing in random idol guests for the sake of viewer ratings when they don't really work with the program or the cast at all. The article uses EXO as an example, saying, "SM's rookie idol group EXO is busy trying to get their faces known wherever they can. Their guesting on 'Immortal Song' should be considered an aegyo, as they've also been on 'We Got Married' with Taemin as well as on 'I Live Alone' as Kang Ta's juniors. EXO fans may be happy to see their oppas everywhere but there's much to be questioned in how much they actually helped Taemin and Kang Ta on their respective shows."

Lee Joon's guesting on 'The Human Condition' is also used as a random guesting that didn't really do much for the show, instead bringing down the cast since they have to adjust to him being there, etc.
