[Instiz Dump] Fandom studies, EXO whereabouts, Zico's doppelganger, Running Man guests

Monday, March 18, 2013  instiz  102 comments

The SHINee fandom has started to study Japanese because of SHINee's increasing activities in Japan. A checklist of important phrases fans must know (TN: don't trust my romanizations, I'm romanizing from Korean Japanese):

- Don't push me (osanaideyo)
- Go back. Don't come to the front. (ushiro ni sagate. maeni susumanaide.)
- Put that down, I can't see (sore jotto sageteyo. mienaindayo)
- Why are you wearing heels to a concert? (koko de high heel damedayo)
- Put down your fan, you b*tch (uchiwafuse kusoama)
- Stop pushing, b*tch (osunjaneyo deme)
- Move your LED, dammit (eruidido kaseyokora)
- Shut up (kuchiba shidamare)
- Stop acting out (hikkondero)
- Wanna die? (korosuzo omeyo)
- You're pissing me off (maji mukatsuku!!!)
