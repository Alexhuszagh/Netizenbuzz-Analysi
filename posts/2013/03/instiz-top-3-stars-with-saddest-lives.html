[Instiz] Top 3 stars with the saddest lives

Thursday, March 07, 2013  hong suk chun, instiz, jun jin, shin eun kyung  149 comments


Jun Jin

1. His father was poor while his mother came from a rich family so as soon as Jun Jin was born, his grandparents took his mother away to live with them so Jun Jin grew up without a mother.

2. The current mother he's living with is his third step-mother and people say that they're still awkward.

3. He decided to become a celebrity to find his real mother, and although he eventually did end up finding her, he found out that she had another son and chose not to contact her because he didn't want to put that son through what he had to grow up with.

4. He used to have severe psychological issues and always lived in fear of dying at any moment. He is currently blind in one eye and his constant worry is becoming fully blind.

5. All of the money he made as a celebrity was lost by his dad Charlie Park by a fraud. He still always names his father as the celebrity he respects the most.

6. Before he enlisted in the army, his agency worked him like a dog. He joined Infinity Challenge at a time when he was in constant fatigue so fans of the variety hated on him... He was eventually hospitalized for being overworked. That agency's CEO was Jung, the man currently arrested for sexually assaulting his trainees.

7. During his time on Dream Team, he ended up injuring his head while showing a tumbling move. The doctors said that it's a miracle he came out of the surgery alive. Four days later, he broke a new record on the show.

8. A kkot-baem hit on him and took a manipulative picture of him that spread... People told him to sue her but he decided to just forgive her.

9. He was born with a weak back so he's always in the hospital for it but he insists on running and dancing if needed for TV shows.

-

Shin Eun Kyung

1. When she was little, her parents forced her into the entertainment industry.

2. She worked like a dog without any rest and all of the money she made was stolen by her parents to pay off their debt.

3. Right when all of their debt was about to be paid off, her parents started a new business and used up all of her contract fee.

4. Because of her image as the 'Mafia's Wife' and the type of characters she played (prostitutes), people didn't want to be associated with her.

5. While filming 'Mafia's Wife 2', a piece of wood broke off and hit her left eye and nearly left her blind.

6. When she was suffering from severe depression, she was caught drunk driving and was almost exiled from the industry.

7. Her son is suffering from an incurable disease.

8. Her former CEO and current husband transferred all of his debt to her.

9. Her agency committed a mistake which they blamed her for... Her agency also never shielded her from the media for it.

10. There were several times where dramas and movies canceled her casting because debt collectors would come to the film sets looking for her.

11. She used to be an alcoholic due to her severe depression but she's fortunately recovered.

-

Hong Suk Chun

1. He was ranked #1 out of the whole school during his high school days but nobody wanted to be his friend because he was gay.

2. He officially came out in 2000 and was promptly exiled from the industry. At a time when even drug abusers would return after a year of hiatus, it took him three years to be allowed on TV again.

3. He lent money to some people that desperately needed it but never ended up getting paid back so he lived without much for a while...

4. Fortunately, his restaurant business hit a success but the people around him continued to use him for their own benefit.

5. He fell in love with a Netherland man and helped him out financially, even paying for him to study abroad. He made the decision to come out because of him but he was betrayed in the end.

6. A US soldier stationed in Korea came to his restaurant drunk one night and set the entire place on fire... He decided to forgive him without taking any compensation for his losses.

7. He says it's difficult for him to date because the entire world knows that he's gay.

8. When his sister was going through a tough divorce, he voluntarily agreed to adopt her two kids as his own legal son and daughter. They are currently studying abroad.

-

Source: Instiz
