[Instiz] Male idols with the most copyrighted songs

Sunday, March 24, 2013  instiz  179 comments

Compilation of male idols with the most songs registered with KOMCA (see the previous female idol list here). This list's criteria required that at least 10 of the songs be self compositions, not just lyric writing. For example, an idol with 50 registered songs wouldn't be on the list if he didn't compose at least 10 out of those 50 songs.

--------------------------



1. G-Dragon: 128 songs

Representative track: 'Lies' - Big Bang
Lyrics: G-Dragon
Composition: G-Dragon
Arrangement: Brave Brothers


2. Moon Hee Jun: 79 songs


3. Kang Ta: 77 songs


4. M (Minwoo): 57 songs

Representative track: 'The M Style (Feat. Tablo)' - M
Lyrics: M
Composition: M


5. Junhyung: 49 songs

Representative track: 'Caffeine' - Yoseob
Lyrics: Kim Tae Joo, Junhyung
Composition: Kim Tae Joo, Junhyung
Arrangement: Kim Tae Joo, Junhyung


6. Zico: 46 songs

Representative track: 'Nillili Mambo' - Block B
Lyrics: Zico
Composition: Zico, Pop Time
Arrangement: Zico, Nusoul, Pop Time


7. TOP: 44 songs


8. Eun Ji Won: 43 songs


9. Eric: 42 songs


10. Jay Park: 34 songs


11. Jaejoong: 32 songs

Representative track: 'In Heaven' - JYJ
Lyrics: Jaejoong
Composition: Jaejoong
Arrangement: BJD, MDS


12. Yonghwa: 31 songs

Representative track: 'Love Girl' - CNBLUE
Lyrics: Han Sung Ho, Yonghwa
Composition: Han Seung Hoon, Yonghwa
Arrangement: Han Seung Hoon, Yonghwa


13. Junsu: 21 songs

Representative track: 'Tarantallegra' - Junsu
Lyrics: Juno
Composition: Junsu


14. Yoochun: 19 songs


15. Jinyoung: 11 songs

Representative track: 'Baby Good Night' - B1A4
Lyrics: Baro, Jinyoung
Composition: Jinyoung
Arrangement: Jinyoung, Good Guy

----------------

Source: Instiz
