Variety viewers get fed up with Kwanghee's use of informal speech to seniors

Monday, January 21, 2013  gary, kwanghee, running man, zea  91 comments


Article: Running Man 'I hate Haha and Gary' the severity of Kwanghee's informal speech use

Source: Sports Chosun via Nate

On 'Running Man', Kwanghee, an 88er, said "I hate Haha and Gary" in informal speech in reference to two people who are 11 and 12 years older than him. The article delves into other instances where Kwanghee spoke in informal speech to seniors on other shows like 'Laws of the Jungle' but managed to avoid controversy because his co-stars covered it up as comedy.

He tried to speak informally to Julien Kang on 'We Got Married' despite Julien Kang showing that he was uncomfortable with it. Panelist Kim Mi Sun saw the scene and said that Kwanghee had also tried to use informal speech with her and that it had shocked her at the time.

The article claims that viewers are done with 'coddling him' now that he's committed the same mistake on 'Running Man', and the article says that he is deserving of the criticism.
