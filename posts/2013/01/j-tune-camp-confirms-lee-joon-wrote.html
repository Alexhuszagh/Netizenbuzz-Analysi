J. Tune Camp confirms Lee Joon wrote the fancafe message

Wednesday, January 23, 2013  lee joon, mblaq, oh yeon seo  49 comments


Article: Lee Joon's agency admits "Lee Joon indeed wrote it"

Source: Sports Chosun via Nate

J. Tune Camp stated, "Yesterday morning, the post written on the official fancafe was indeed written by Lee Joon. Lee Joon was in the middle of working out a compromise with the agency because of his tight schedule."

Lee Joon had complained about his tight schedule, saying it was difficult for him to truly treasure each experience on stage and be genuine in his interest and work. The agency admitted to not being able to work with him on the schedule, leading Lee Joon to feel psychologically and physically stressed and tired.

The post was written under such conditions and the agency admits to taking the full blame. "Although we were shocked, it is something we can understand as a member of our agency family." They promised to manage his schedule in a way that allows Lee Joon to take care of his condition so that he can focus on putting his best into every work and stage.
