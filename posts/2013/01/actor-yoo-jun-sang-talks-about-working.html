Actor Yoo Jun Sang talks about working with idols in musicals

Thursday, January 17, 2013  yoo jun sang  81 comments


Article: Yoo Jun Sang "Idols advancing into the musical field? Just arrive on time"

Source: Newsen via Nate

"I've worked with a lot of idols and they're great. I don't reprimand them if they come out on time and try their best. In the musical world, it's an unwritten rule that you're supposed to be at the theater three hours before the show starts. I look at my watch and write down the names of those that don't come by the three hour mark. In the order that they come in, I dunk their faces in water or in the winter, make them rub snow all over themselves. They need to know the importance of time management."
