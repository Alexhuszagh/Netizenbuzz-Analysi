TV: [Spoilers!!!] Yawang angers viewers with extreme and unrealistic plot

Friday, January 25, 2013  korean dramas, kwon sang woo, soo ae, tv, yawang, yunho  91 comments


Article: Viewers angered over Soo Ae's unreasonable character in 'Yawang'

Source: News 24 via Naver

Article that talks about how viewers have been expressing their anger/criticisms over how the drama is going too extreme with the plotline, especially with Soo Ae's character.

[Spoilers!!!] In the drama, Soo Ae abandons her husband, Kwon Sang Woo, and her daughter to chase after a dream job that she finds out she lost because she didn't return Yunho's feelings.

She decides to follow Yunho and leave Korea when Yunho has to study abroad after an injury that leaves him unable to pursue a sports career. Kwon Sang Woo must then go back to working at a host bar to support the family and let Soo Ae do as she wishes.

Soo Ae lives off of the money Kwon Sang Woo sends her and begins dating Yunho, completely forgetting about her family back home.

When a motherly figure to Kwon Sang Woo falls short on her hospital bills, he calls Soo Ae and asks if she can rest a semester because he doesn't have enough to cover both her tuition and the hospital bill. Soo Ae declines, leading Kwon Sang Woo to sell his body to make more money.

Viewers are calling Soo Ae's character a psychopath and refusing to watch anymore because of the extreme plot.
