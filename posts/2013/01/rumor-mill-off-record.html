[Rumor Mill] Off the Record

Thursday, January 03, 2013  off the record, rumor mill  144 comments


1. Actor B, who is at the height of his career right now, has reportedly caught the celebrity disease. B was a star in a very popular drama recently and has been rising as a bluechip in the CF world thanks to the love of his fans. The problem lies in his difference in attitude. Usually known for his refreshing personality, he's maintained a positive relationship with broadcasting officials until he recently threw a fit in while at a private recording for a music program. He had asked if the show could go out of their way to make it public specifically for him, which producers could not comply with. In the end, he threw a fit with them.

2. Hallyu star C is currently starring in a popular drama and is regarded as the figure responsible for the high viewer ratings. Unfortunately, despite his lead role status and importance to the drama, he's said to be receiving subpar treatment from those around him. Despite it all, he remained nice towards them until it finally became too much for him when he wasn't invited to any year-end event, causing him to break out in tears. His company had to step in for him to get him a few invites and onto nomination lists as a supporting actor. His pride had already been too hurt at this point so he asked to be removed. C had actually received invites from 20 other Chinese award ceremonies but had declined them all for his domestic schedules and to keep his loyalty to the broadcast officials he thought he could trust. Unfortunately, the year-end did not turn out how he expected.

3. Former child actress E is making a round on the gossip table among insiders due to her personality. She makes too many requests for events to comply with. While at the music video film set for another singer in Thailand, she requested a specific brand of water that had staff running around in search for. Even veteran actors that she recently starred in said nothing when producers begrudgingly complied with all of her requests because they didn't want to deal with the drama that would ensue if they didn't. Although E has made a successful transformation from a child to an adult actress, she might need more than just her skills to continue her success.
