Source: Sports Seoul via Nate

1. [+575, -30] This pisses me off the more I think about it. I remember driving down to my relative's over a holiday break. Right when I got on the highway, someone in the car in front of me stuck out their hand and threw away a roll of tissue paper. Before I could even process what it was, the tissue was already covering my windshield. Thank god it was early morning then because I don't even know what would've happened if it was at night. I thought I was going to die at that point so I slammed the brakes and swerved on to the side of the road. I was lucky that the road was mostly empty because if I had braked like that in the middle of the day, me and a few other cars would've lost our lives that day. Ever since then, I've wanted to break the wrist of anyone that carelessly throws things out of their cars. Didn't we all learn not to even stick any of our body parts out the windows?

2. [+527, -23] It's obvious that the only ones defending her are thoughtless teens who've never once driven before. When you're speeding in a highway, even a small rock picks up enough force to crack your windshield. There was even in the news of a beetle shattering a windshield and causing an accident. Considering that she's a celebrity and she has schedules to get to, I don't doubt that the van was speeding over the limit. Moreover, if the bag had ripped, the oranges would not have simply dropped to the floor but instead flown onto the windshield of the car behind them. After that, a path to hell just opens for anyone unfortunate to have their view obscured by it.

3. [+363, -61] Jung Eunji's three strikes
1. North Korean flag (national security law)
2. Challenge 1000 Songs (disrespectful action)
3. Orange controversy (traffic law violation)

4. [+61, -5] Anyone that defends this, just please stay off the road. It's because of idiots like you that oranges turn into weapons on the road

5. [+44, -1] Anyone that drives will know just how much goosebumps this situation gives them;;; My hands shake even when a piece of paper flies over my windshield, I can't imagine anything worse..
