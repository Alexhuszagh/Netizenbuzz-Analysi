[Rumor Mill] Celebrity soldier shooting a CF during service, cheating relationships, and violence issues

Sunday, January 13, 2013  off the record, rumor mill, whisper gossip  181 comments


1. This month has been a scandalous month for celebrity soldiers, with the industry buzzing about the perks they've been receiving. While plenty of soldiers that managed to remain out of the spotlight remain in a nervous state, a recently enlisted popular idol group member 'F' was said to have shot a CF while serving in the army. F was said to have worn a wig while out during his break to shoot his CF. As the CF is only being broadcast in Japan and not Korea, it has yet to be discovered by the eyes of netizens. Apparently it's common for celebrities to continue their promotional activities while still serving in the army. They just simply say it was recorded prior to their enlistment.

2. Top star 'C' who's been in and out of the media for his dating rumors has been discovered to actually be cheating on his special lady, D. Although C spent the Christmas holiday with D, he was actually spotted on a date with E for the New Year's. Unlike her younger age, E has been receiving much love for her mature looks and has a history of breaking the hearts of thousands of fans for admitting to dating another popular singer. E was probably shocked to see recent news confirming that D and C were indeed dating... Perhaps she now knows how devastated those fans felt when she broke their hearts?

3. A rookie actress is said to be wielding the power equivalent to any mafia. A was caught in a relationship with both her non-celebrity boyfriend 'B' and her road manager. Although she had promised her road manager that she would marry him, she went back on the promise to stay true to her relationship with B. She didn't know how to break it to her manager so she told B to beat him up for her, which he complied with. The manager ended up a complete mess once he was done with him but A got what she wanted in the end.

4. Middle-aged veteran actor C who's the #1 most requested celebrity in the industry for his unique looks and acting talents was recently spotted beating up junior actor D at a night out drinking. D is an actor who mainly focuses on dramas as opposed to movies and isn't that far off in age compared to C. C not only beat D up in the pub, but even went outside and crushed D's luxury foreign car. When D tried to fight back, it only angered C further, resulting in more violence. C's turn in personality once alcohol gets into his system is not a secret in the industry. He turns aggressive once drunk and picks a fight with anyone around him. His co-stars, well aware of such habits, had even clicked their tongues at him in interviews, leading him to declare that he'd stay away from alcohol for health reasons... but it obviously seems he's back to his old habits.
