Article: [Exclusive] "Save me" cries... Third grades of Soonchun elementary school under controversy for school violence

Source: No Cut News via Nate

Ten students in third grade bullied a fellow classmate 'A' (10 years old) for 10 months and were caught once their homeroom teacher discovered a video of the group abusing her on a cellphone.

Her mother wrote on an internet community board: "Since the beginning of the school year, I noticed that my daughter had been acting weird. I had a bad feeling by summertime that I sent a text to her homeroom teacher asking her to look over her. Soon after, however, my child came home with bruises on her limbs and it was only recently that the truth was revealed.

The video was not a video of physical abuse but absolute torture. Despite her pleas not to film her, the students grabbed her by the head and shoved the phone in her face, spraying water over her, punching her back, and forcing her to kneel while they screamed swears at her.

In the video, she desperately clung to a cubby  so that they would not pull her out of the classroom. In another instance, they used the teacher's stick to slap her hands with when she was drawing. The cries of my child asking to be saved still echoes in my head."

According to the parents, the school had refused to show the video to them until the case was reported to the school violence center and they were forced to reveal it.
