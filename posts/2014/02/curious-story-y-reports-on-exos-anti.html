'Curious Story Y' reports on EXO's anti fan scandal

Friday, February 28, 2014  exo  330 comments


Article: 'Curious Story Y' reports on 'Kim Yeo Shin EXO terror scandal', turns out to be a junior high school anti fan

Source: TV Report via Nate

The report covered a scandal where someone named 'Kim Yeo Shin' threatened to terror EXO, leading fans to trend search words asking the members to be careful of taking drinks from fans to protect them.

The anti-fan claimed that she was responsible for the poisoned drink against Yunho in 2006 and that she had done it because her parents were killed due to sasaeng fans chasing TVXQ in 2007.

Investigation proved that she was using a fake ID and that there were no fatal accidents on the date she claimed. Furthermore, her parents supposedly died after the poisoning had already taken place.

She never showed up on the date she promised to 'terror EXO' and instead left a long apology letter claiming to be an average junior high school student who started an anti EXO blog for the attention. She apologized for her actions and said that she will not do it again.
