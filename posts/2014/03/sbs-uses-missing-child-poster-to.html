SBS uses a missing child poster to promote their drama "God's Gift"

Wednesday, March 05, 2014  god's gift, korean dramas, tv  249 comments


Article: "Please find this missing child" SBS drama's 'ridiculous' promotion controversy

Source: Yonhap News via Nate

The drama "God's Gift" tweeted a poster of a missing child, which spread on SNS. The problem is that the poster isn't actually reporting a missing child but a virtual character from the drama, 9 year old Han Saet Byul. An SBS rep said that they were intending to show that SNS can be used for good deeds like spreading awareness on missing children but netizens thought it was a misuse of a missing child's poster, which people might mistake to be real.
