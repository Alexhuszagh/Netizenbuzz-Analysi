Kim Gura names fandom influence on digital + music show wins as an adverse effect

Thursday, July 03, 2014  kim gura  398 comments


Article: Kim Gura, "Certain songs winning #1 on digital charts + music shows is an adverse effect of fandoms"

Source: OSEN via Naver

Kim Gura: "Fandoms have grown to the point where they're able to control even the broadcast companies. Nowadays, songs will shoot up the rankings at once so there are a lot of nugu songs winning #1 because fans will come together to stream all at once.

There are songs where fans will make win #1 on music shows and that song is never heard of again. I can't help but say this is an adverse effect of fandoms."

Lee Sang Min: "With all of this effort fans put in, singers must treat their fans nice. In the past, promotions were up to the company but fandoms do it all for them these days."

Kim Gura: "There are even some fans who study hard to become scriptwriters and PDs themselves. 'Starwatcher' scriptwriter Hwang Sun Young is a fan of Shin Hye Sung and PD Shin Hyo Jung is Lee Seung Gi's fan."
