Brand New Music's Rhymer predicts idols who can't produce for themselves will be booted by next year

Monday, November 24, 2014  brand new music, rhymer  194 comments


Article: [K-Pop Producers] Rhymer "Idols who rely on their composers will disappear soon"

Source: OSEN via Naver

Q. "It seems to be the trend lately for idol groups with the talent to produce themselves being at the top, like B2ST or Block B."

Rhymer. "I predict that idol groups who aren't able to produce for themselves will all be dead by next year. There will be less and less room for idol groups who only receive tracks from their composers and reiterate it like machines. Before, idols were praised for releasing self-compositions, but now, idol groups that can produce for themselves with bigger and better ideas will increase."

Q. "There definitely has been too many idol groups who have everything produced for them."

Rhymer. "There are kids who literally say, 'I don't know anything. Please help me.' When I ask them their opinion on something, they reply back, 'I don't know. Decide for me.' I find that there's a problem with that."
