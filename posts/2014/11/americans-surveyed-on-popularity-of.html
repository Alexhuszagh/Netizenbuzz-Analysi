Americans surveyed on the popularity of Korean dramas

Sunday, November 23, 2014  korean dramas, tv  209 comments

Article: Korean dramas 'popular' in America... wide range of viewers

Source: Yonhap News via Naver

Survey completed done by the American headquarters of Korea Creative Content Agency (KOCCA) revealed the following results: 2,304 participants said they had watched a Korean drama with English subtitles at least once in America.

Age
16~20 years old: 35.9%
20~25 years old: 22.6%
46 and older: 10.3%
26~30 years old: 9.9%
21~35 years old: 6.7%

Ethnicity
Asian (Chinese, Filipino, Vietnamese, etc): 29%
Hispanic: 18.9%
African: 9%
Korean: 4.4%

Service used:
Dramafever: 55.7%
Viki: 25.7%
Hulu: 4.2%
Netflix: 1.7%

Years spent watching Korean dramas:
Over 3 years: 59.2%
1~3 years: 28.5%
6 months~1 year: 7.4%

Favorite genre:
Romantic comedy: 72%
Melo: 12.9%
Historical: 6.9%
Crime/Action: 6.6%

Favorite top dramas:
Boys Over Flowers
You Who Came From the Stars
Secret Garden
Coffee Prince
Master's Sun
