Can YG continue their 100% success streak this year?

Sunday, November 09, 2014  yg entertainment  599 comments


Article: "All albums, all #1"... 2014, can YG continue their 100% win streak?

Source: TV Report via Naver

1. [+2,560, -174] SM completely broke down this year, making YG stand out all the more by comparison

2. [+2,201, -142] YG showed that they're not just a hip hop company but one that aims for a variety of music through AkMu, Lee Hi, and more.

3. [+1,950, -132] SM and YG both had many scandals this year but the biggest difference is in the success of their artists... and YG's all succeeded.

4. [+1,688, -117] This is the first year in many that YG probably made more than SM. YG had no failures this year while everything from SM failed.

5. [+269, -14] Didn't realize that Epik High didn't win that many trophies compared to how well they did popularity wise...

6. [+266, -19] 2NE1 in February, AkMu in April, Taeyang in June, Winner in August, and Epik High in October... all five teams released official albums that lined up on the digital charts. YG's just too strong digitally...!

7. [+239, -11] YG may drive their fans up the wall with their planning but once their songs are actually released, the quality is always outstanding... Anyway, seems Big Bang's comeback is impossible this year. Wishing Hi Suhyun a daebak as well.

8. [+228, -12] Taking away all the scandals, YG is above and beyond ahead of the other agencies this year. AkMu and Epik High's album qualities were outstanding. I never buy albums but I had to buy theirs just to own it. If only YG could manage their artists better...
