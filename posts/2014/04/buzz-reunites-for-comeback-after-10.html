    This band was one of Korea's most popular bands back in the mid-2000s (before idol groups became dominant), supposedly with a fandom that was just as large as SNSD's during its heydays.     Lot of I-fans probably never heard, but this band is easily a huge nostalgia to Korean youths now in their 20s and 30s. They were HUGE back then.

    With the original vocalist Min Kyunghoon being the last to finish his service, so this is something that's been long anticipated.
