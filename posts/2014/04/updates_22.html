Article: Helmsman "Strange people... I am confident" unashamed

Source: TV Daily via Nate

Helmsman who abandoned the passengers and escaped the ferry immediately after the accident said, "The captain told us to escape so the ten of us escaped through the portside. I didn't see who escaped first but I think I was the 7th or 8th person to escape. He ordered for us to escape."

When asked if the passengers received the same orders, "The chief mate was given orders and it's his job to contact the broadcasting room to make the announcement but I don't know the exact process."

When asked if crew are supposed to leave immediately when ordered to by the captain, he said, "According to the manual, you're supposed to save the old, weak, and the children first. As soon as orders are given, crewmen are supposed to make sure that all of the passengers have escaped, make another round with the captain to make sure again before getting off, and the captain is then supposed to make a final check to see if anyone is still on board before getting off last."

When asked why he didn't follow the manual then, he said, "How could I follow the manual in that situation? How could I make it to the cabins? You people are so strange. Who is going to broadcast this on TV? See, none of you are replying. I'll stand up confidently so release this on broadcast as is."
