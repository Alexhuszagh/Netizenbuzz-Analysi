[Pann] A+ complaints about J. Tune's treatment of MBLAQ

Monday, June 16, 2014  j tune, mblaq, pro c  159 comments

Pann: [+446, -5] The atrocious acts of a company that's more like its group's own anti

This is a collection of J. Tune (read: J. Trash) atrocious acts.

1. Recruit of chapter 3 of fanclub: On May 10, 2013, a notice was put up about the recruit of chapter 3 of A+ fanclub. Promotions end on August 2014. As of June 2014, A+ who have applied for a chapter three membership have not received their cards or any benefits.

2. Pro C scandal: Fans already know what happened but there is a junior group named Pro C. They put up an apology post but it's more like a threat. MBLAQ probably never received an apology from those Pro C idiots. As more posts up putting J. Tune down came up on their official fancafe, they blocked off Pro C's fancafe entirely.

What happened during the scandal: On Feb. 26, 2014, 100 A+ were able to join MBLAQ at their training room to monitor their new track. They took away their cellphones to prevent song leaks. Pro C was there to collect the phones and in front of 100 MBLAQ fans, they made comments putting MBLAQ down and verbally sexually harassed the A+ members.

Collection of what they said: "I bet MBLAQ won #2 by kissing (sleeping with women)", "A collection of devils (referring to A+)", "Shut up you b*tches", "How much will we make if we sell off all your phones?", "Probably not much even if you sell it to China".

J. Tune posted an apology that fans rejected.

3. Concert cancellation scandal: MBLAQ went to perform in Peru on June 13th, 2014. The concert was ridiculously canceled not the day before but the day it was to take place. 9,000 fans were scheduled to come but promoters said they were planning to reschedule to January 2015. J. Tune never apologized or confirmed any further schedules.

GO tweeted in response: "There is nothing we can do. I'm so angry that I can't express myself in any way... Who will know what happened here? Why are we here for a concert that isn't prepared... for what reason? This is the most embarrassed I've been as a singer."

4. Official goods: It's been six years since MBLAQ debuted. However, they still don't have an official cheer stick or official fan merchandise. There are even talks of the company collecting money to make said goods that never came out There probably won't be an official cheer stick since MBLAQ's official color, pearl chocolate, is difficult to illuminate.

5. No show events: The company puts out a bunch of events on Twitter but the winner is never known. For example, they never announced a winner for the 2013 Smoky Girl capture event and swept it under the rug. The winner for the 'come here' event still hasn't received her prize.

6. Kim Il Sung scandal: In 2012, MBLAQ's manager Kim Il Sung called fans 'stupid fangirls', variations of 'idiot', etc without an apology. We wonder if he is still working at J. Tune?

7. Media play: There is never any media play for MBLAQ. It was so bad that last year, rumors say an Lee Joon fanpage did more in promoting MBLAQ than J. Tune themselves.

8. Two X: Their debut song didn't even do that bad and for a rookie girl group, they did okay but J. Tune forgets about them to make another rookie Pro C. Two X probably could've won #1 if they had just focused on them instead.

9. Coordis: So many things to say. They dress the boys in dye-less pants, reuse outfits, dress them in hot clothing in the summer and cold clothing in the winter, accidentally burn their comeback outfits, and obviously put them in sizes that don't fit at all. Some of the members are swimming in their outfits.

10. Official fancafe: Nobody manages the official fancafe. Spam ads run rampant and notices and schedule additions are never put up on time.

11. Composer: This is a bit of a sensitive issue but there are talks of GO having to hire a composer through his own network of connections.
