[Pann] One father studies for the college entrance exam for eight years

Wednesday, June 04, 2014  nonentertainment, pann  67 comments

At a home in Kangwondo

My name is No Hee Joo. I am a 20 year old preparing to retake the college entrance exam.

I would like to introduce my special father. (No Tae Kwon, 57 years old)

These are headphones my father made himself.

With these, he listens to exam lectures on EBS every day.

As a junior high school graduate and a manual laborer, is it because he wants to go to college now, albeit late?

No

My father is the teacher of us two brothers.

In junior high, I suffered from severe atopy while my hyung was lost in gaming addiction.

We were not able to advance to high school.

Instead of going to school, our father would teach himself the material

and teach it to us.



"Because I had nothing and had learned nothing, I kept reading and reading, and writing and writing."

"Once I read material over to the point of the text being erased from wear, the content naturally became memorized in my head."

Hyung was admitted to Seoul University's business administration department in 2010. (Noh Dong Joo, 22 years old)

And I am studying hard to be as good as, no, to exceed, my hyung.

My father has been studying for the college entrance exam for eight years now.
