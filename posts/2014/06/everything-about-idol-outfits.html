Everything about idol outfits

Monday, June 23, 2014    344 comments


Article: [B class question] How are idol outfits made and what happens to idol outfits after promotions are over?

Source: Kyunghyang News via Naver

A visual director draws out the outfit concepts which is then passed over to stylists to add details on fabric and other accessories before finally being created either by the stylist themselves or a professional responsible for producing outfits for singers.

After use, outfits are stored in the company's closet. Idols do not reuse the outfits. If the outfits are needed for concerts or other fan events after promotions end, they are produced all over again for wear. SM Ent said, "We rarely discard the outfits and either keep them to open an exhibit or put them up for charity auctions."
