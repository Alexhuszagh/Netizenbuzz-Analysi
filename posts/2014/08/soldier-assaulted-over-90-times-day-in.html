victim's assailants

Source: Pann (graphic pictures)

- Beat while force fed frozen foods, causing him to choke on his own throw up, eventually leading to brain death
- Taken to the hospital but passed away the next day
- Reasons for assault: for not replying properly, for replying in a small/slow voice, for being late (due to a leg injury resulting from assault), for not listening, for sleeping, for passing out (after being beaten to the point of uncontrollable drool/urination)...
