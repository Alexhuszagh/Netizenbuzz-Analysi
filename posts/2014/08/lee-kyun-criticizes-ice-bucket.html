Lee Kyun criticizes, "Ice bucket challenge? You don't even know what Lou Gehrig's disease is..."

Wednesday, August 20, 2014  ice bucket challenge, lee kyun  197 comments


Article: Lee Kyun criticizes, "Ice bucket challenge? You don't even know what Lou Gehrig's disease is..."

Source: X Sports News via Nate

"Ice bucket videos are uploaded like a trend lately. I acknowledge their intentions but I wonder if they're doing it because they know anything about the Lou Gehrig's disease? The ice water is to express the contraction of the muscles and its pain but everyone seems to be enjoying it too much for fun. Don't do it if it's for that reason. At least know this as a minimum. The disease that Kim Myung Min suffered from in the movie 'Lover By My Side' is the Lou Gehrig's disease. It makes me uncomfortable for me to see stars pour ice water over themselves and enjoy it... like it's refreshing.

To all of the patients, please find strength. I'll be donating to those of you in pain... It disappoints me that I never hear these words. I was going to shoot an ice bucket video myself... but I know it's going to turn into shameless self promotion so I decided against it."
