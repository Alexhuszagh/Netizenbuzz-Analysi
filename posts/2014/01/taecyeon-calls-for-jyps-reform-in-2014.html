Taecyeon calls for JYP's reform in 2014

Friday, January 03, 2014  2pm, taecyeon  167 comments


Article: Ok Taecyeon advises JYP "In need of reform in 2014... Internal development needed"

Source: TV Report via Naver

"If our company hopes to become the best once again in 2014, then we need to bring forth the strength of not our producer but all our company's employees. For the unappreciated JYP staffs but who deserve the actual credit.

We need to become a company where the staff don't just check in and check out but where staff actually want to work. If the people working at an entertainment company are not entertained themselves, how can we expect to entertain the public? We shouldn't be focused on being a top three agency but rather our internal development.

If I were to say that I advanced without any management or support, what does that make the people who managed and supported me? How can I expect my fans to understand a company that even I, a celebrity who is a part of the company, cannot understand? JYP needs reform in 2014."
