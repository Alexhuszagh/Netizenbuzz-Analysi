Article: Actress A, 'ostracized' for throwing fits and refusing bed scene with top male actor

Source: Newsen via Nate

Actress A known for arriving late to scheduled activities is currently being ostracized by senior actors for her disrespectful actions.

Although she's known for her pretty looks and positive image, actress A has reportedly never arrived to a film set on time, forcing seniors to wait for her for sometimes up to three hours.

She was found to have refused to film a scene involving a kiss and bed scene, both of which were pre-planned and written in the scenario she agreed to. A claimed that she would not do it no matter what, forcing reps into a mental breakdown. Her co-star was a handsome top male actor.

Producers eventually gave in and asked her for a kiss scene which would only show her face but A demanded to use a body double. She also refused to have her face shown. According to reps, the scenario was eventually edited to leave out the kiss and bed scene altogether.

A rep explained, "She always wants to receive the princess treatment on set. She'll sometimes even leave the set and go home if filming is delayed or she isn't treated like a princess. Even senior actors who are known for their outstanding personalities have given up on her, leaving her an outcast."
