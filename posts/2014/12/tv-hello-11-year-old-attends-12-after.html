Article: 'Hello' 11 year old student attends 12 after school academies... "My health isn't what it used to be"

 Source: OSEN via Nate

She asked her mom if she could cut back but her mom refused. Her dad said she could quit them all but her mom does not listen, saying, "I'm sure any mother will know that this (amount of academies) is just average.
