Plastic surgery on the rise after the end of college entrance exams

Tuesday, December 16, 2014  nonentertainment, society  204 comments


Article: From plastic surgery part time jobs to plastic surgery loans and even plastic surgery fund... is my daughter doing it too?

Source: Money Today via Nate

Article talks about how now that college entrance exams are over, high school students are saving up to get plastic surgery before they enter college. Many are working part time jobs and plastic surgery clinics are offering loans and special discounts based on your college entrance exam test grades.

Plastic surgery rises by 50% from December to February with students scrambling to change their looks before the new semester.
