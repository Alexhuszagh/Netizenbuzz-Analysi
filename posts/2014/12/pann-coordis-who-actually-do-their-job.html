[Pann] Coordis who actually do their job

Saturday, December 27, 2014  infinite, pann  201 comments




A lot of Shin Hae Chul fans expressed their gratitude over how perfect INFINITE's outfits were for their remembrance performance. Red on black were the theme colors for his song NEXT and fans were touched that INFINITE expressed that. 



A lot of idols often get in trouble for performing in heels at soccer stadiums and scuffing up the grass but INFINITE's coordis always make it a point to switch them into sneakers to keep the grass clean. For their performance at a Samsung Bluewings game, the coordis even changed the boys into blue theme colored suits. 


INFINITE had to perform in Yokohama over the Chuseok holiday and the members suggested that they wear hanboks. The coordis listened and dressed them accordingly.





A lot of their outfits have immense attention to detail and are hand crafted works by the coordis themselves. The coordis also take into consideration the stature of each member, like dressing Sungyeol in long coats to make his tall height stand out and dressing shorter members like Dongwoo in rider jackets to make up for his lacking proportion. 


Coordis also dress them with consideration to their choreography. To make sure the members aren't distracted by their ties flopping around, they'll pick out shirts that have tie fixes sewn into them. 


The coordis also listen to the members when they give their opinions. For a part in 'Man In Love' where the members hit their jackets back, L had suggested that their coats add wrinkling to make the effect look more powerful. The coordis went ahead and stitched in the ripple effect on their jackets. 

The members also recycled their outfits at the '2013 MAMA', and despite never having worn them in two years, the clothes looked like they were well taken care of, showing the amount of care the coordis put in their work.
