Pann: Any of you suffering from sharing names with a celebrity

I'm a sophomore at a co-ed high school and I'm suffering because my name's Park Chanyeol. When I had no clue who EXO was, I looked up his name because I was curious but the difference in our looks........ anyway, I think I'll be suffering for the rest of my life.
