Saturday, December 27, 2014    317 comments


Article: Singers choose the year's best singer + song + album

Source: MBN via Nate

Participating singers: Postman, Huh Gak, Orange Caramel, Sunny Hill, FTTS, BTS Boys, 4minute, B2ST, BtoB, AOA, CNBLUE, TEEN TOP, 100%, MIB, Crush, Roy Kim, Hello Venus, Jung Jun Young, Park Boram, Hong Dae Kwang, Topdogg, Block B, LOVELYZ, A Pink, SISTAR, Junggigo, Boyfriend, Girl's Day, VIXX, B1A4, Bestie, Got7, Taetiseo, Taemin.

Best Singer of the Year:
1. god (23 votes)
2. Seo Taiji (10 votes), A Pink (10 votes), SISTAR (10 votes), B2ST (10 votes)

Best Song of the Year:
1. Soyu x Junggigo - Some (49 votes)
2. Taeyang - Eyes, Nose, Lips (30 votes)
3. Park Hyoshin - Wild Flower (7 votes), Block B - HER (7 votes), 2PM - Go Crazy (7 votes)

Best Album of the Year:
1. Taeyang - Rise (37 votes)
2. IU - A Flower Bookmark (24 votes)
3. Epik High - Shoebox (14 votes)
