MIB's Sims joins the idol diss train?

Wednesday, December 17, 2014  bobby, ikon, mib, sims  460 comments


Article: Idol, a diss world of their own (Summary)

Source: Dispatch via Nate

Sims -> Bobby

In the song '...To' (released 12/12/14), he said in his lyrics, "I'm still Piglet next to teddy bear Pooh who's still sucking on honey"

- Dispatch's interpretation: Bobby said his favorite item was a Pooh bear on Mix & Match. 'To suck on honey' is an expression of Bobby receiving the support of a large scale company.

"Shut up and to all of the tone deaf rappers up on stage~ find strength"

- Dispatch's interpretation: A counter diss to Bobby calling idol rappers tone deaf.

"Can't you even feel the words you spit out? Battle rap is all ruined"

- Dispatch's interpretation: A criticism of YG's characteristic rap style of slurring words along with criticisms on Bobby's pronunciation problems.

"I rapped on 'Trot X' too"

- Dispatch's interpretation: Vasco had told Bobby on Show Me the MOney 3 that he "sounded like he was doing hip hop on 'Trot X'". Sims has experience guesting on 'Trot X'. A warning to Bobby to not judge someone's rap talent by their stage alone.

"I'm allergic against the XX who hate on idols for no reason"

- Dispatch's interpretation: Sims is against the stereotype against idols and how they're assumed to be untalented.
