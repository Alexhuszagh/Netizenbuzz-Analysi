[Rumor Mill] Popular girl group member's top star disease + plans to leave for acting

Monday, December 08, 2014  rumor mill  551 comments

Article: Manager reveals girl group A's 'top star disease'

Source: Wiki Tree via Instiz

Girl group member 'A' has recently been promoting actively as a solo singer but it's been reported that she's putting those around in her a difficult situation with her top star disease.

On the 8th, Newsen revealed, "After acting in a drama and movie, girl group member A has become cocky. She acts like she's the mightiest and one of the only top stars in the world." According to her main manager, A had declared, "I can't sing or dance so I'll quit my singing career and focus on my acting." She then cut complete contact with her group members.

The manager added, "She's going to get into a huge scandal at this rate." A's mother even went as far as to visit the mother of top idol group member B to ask for her consultation.

-

A previous article from October about the same member:

Girl group member G is said to be quite happy with herself after the success of her recent acting work. Known as the most popular member in her group, she's currently getting her name out there through various works on her own. It seems, however, that she'd rather focus completely on the path of acting and give up singing entirely. Her parents have been heavily involved and recently sought the help of miss A's Suzy's parents and asked questions on her income distribution and acting promotions.
