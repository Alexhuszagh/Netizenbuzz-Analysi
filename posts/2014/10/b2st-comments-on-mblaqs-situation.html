B2ST comments on MBLAQ's situation

Sunday, October 19, 2014  b2st, mblaq  70 comments


Article: B2ST on 'at risk of disbandment' MBLAQ, "It's upsetting and we hope that they resolve everything" support

Source: Mydaily via Naver

"We hope that everything's resolved well. We were surprised at the news, and it's not a topic that we have any right to say anything about. We think it's because their contracts are expired. We are close with the members, but it is a sensitive issue so we can't ask anything more than that.

Since our debuts, we've been compared to them a lot and we're very grateful for them. Because of the constant comparisons, we received a lot of help from them during our debut. It's upsetting and we hope everything's resolved well."
