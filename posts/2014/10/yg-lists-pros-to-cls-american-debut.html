YG lists the pros to CL's American debut

Friday, October 17, 2014  2ne1, cl  343 comments


Article: 'Looks, language, vocals...' CL with the pros, YG on CL's American advancement

Source: TV Report via Naver

Reps, "First of all, there are a lot of pros. CL can both sing and rap. More than anything, there is a scarcity of female rappers in the American music scene. Despite CL being from a girl group, she has a tough and powerful image. She also has an Asian face and is capable of infinite transformations. She can speak four languages (English, Japanese, French, Korean) so there are no restrictions to her expressions in both thought and on the stage.

CL's debut is being made possible by their request and we hope that people think positively of her attempt instead of the results. CL will be our second advancement in America following Psy. We're all curious as to what kind of reaction the American public will give to CL's uniqueness."
