Dongwoon reveals B2ST also share income from individual activities

Wednesday, October 22, 2014  b2st, dongwoon  194 comments


Article: 'Radio Star' Son Dongwoon, "B2ST's individual income is also divided 1/n... decided by show of hands"

Source: Newsen via Nate

"We still divide our individual income 1/n but 98% of B2ST's income comes from group activities. Honestly, individual activities like going on TV don't amount to much. Hyunseung hyung's Troublemaker promos did not make much because the expenses for clothing and album production were shared. Our income mostly comes from concerts, events, and CFs."

When asked if any member with more individual activities would have a problem, he replied, "That's why a year ago, we talked again about our income distribution. The company asked what we wanted to do so we decided by a show of hands and agreed on dividing it. My musical income was also shared with the members."
