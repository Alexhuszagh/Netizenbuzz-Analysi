    Why should someone have to risk their own lives entering a dangerous situation - a leaning and sinking ship filling up with water - to rescue them when the passengers should have been able to get out of the ship on their own? Everyone should know that the first thing you do on a sinking ship is get on a lifeboat. That's what the lifeboats are for. They're not for decoration.


    And the lifeboats are on the top deck, not in the cabins below the deck. What did all those ship sinking movies show if it weren't scenes of people entering lifeboats? If those people had thought for themselves for ten seconds, they could have realized that being in the cabins was not the best thing to do. I'm not blaming them because I could have done the same in that situation, but I am emphasizing that thinking for yourself is the best thing to do in an emergency situation.     If you are in that situation and have thought about it and have come to the conclusion that following the authorities' orders is the best thing to do, then do that.
