Gyuri apologizes to fans regarding KARA's new member addition

Monday, May 12, 2014  gyuri, kara  205 comments


Article: KARA leader Park Gyuri, "Sorry for hurting you..." expresses feelings on new member addition

Source: Sports Donga via Naver

"Our Kamilias are always getting hurt.

I'm sorry.

There are so many things I want to say, misunderstandings I want to solve, and words I want to console you with..

but it isn't easy to put them down in text.

I also think that the original meaning of my words will be misinterpreted, leading to more wounds and difficulties to my people.

I've always been in a position where I couldn't speak out easily but departing from the fact that I'm a celebrity and whatnot, I'm sorry to everyone for hurting you.

There are a lot of hurtful talks about KARA's existence and lack of confidence, etc...

but we will have to show that we are different from what you are thinking... That's our job... isn't it?

For now, I'm just sorry for hurting you.

I'm sorry.

For what's left of today, I hope you have a good day..."
