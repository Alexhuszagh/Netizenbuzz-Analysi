Raina posts a long apology to After School fans regarding their behavior

Wednesday, May 28, 2014  after school, raina  109 comments


Article: After School fans are angry... Raina apologizes, "Sorry for hurting you"

Source: Sports World via Nate

At the idol futsal competition on the 26th, After School's fans felt that the members/Pledis were not respectful to their fans. They did not greet them upon entering and did not tell the fans that they would not be participating in the ending recording and just left.

Fans said, "Fan service is not an obligation but the agency should not ignore the fans outright. We waited for hours on hard chairs and the agency did not tell us anything during the wait. There is a clear communication issue within Pledis."

Major fansites then unified to temporarily close down until the agency cleans up their act.
