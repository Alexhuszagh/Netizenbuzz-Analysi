College launches investigation into severe school bullying case

Monday, June 22, 2015  nonentertainment  190 comments


Article: [Exclusive] "Put a rag in his mouth and beat him" Kyungbuk College A, 5 students charged with assaulting fellow classmate

Source: Chosun Ilbo via Naver

A post was made on an online community site that revealed a group of students at a college dorm was beating up a disabled classmate. The post was made by the victim's father's friend and quickly gained traction online.

Once investigations were launched, it was found that five students had harassed the disabled student in various ways, for example locking him in his dorm for 4 days, tying his hands back and taping them onto a plastic hanger before beating him, pulling his penis, preventing him from going to the bathroom, forcing him to stay awake by making him sit a certain way, shoving a wet rag in his mouth and taping him from head to toe before beating him with a clothes hanger, etc.

The reason for the abuse was claimed to be because "he didn't pay back the money he owed". The victim had apparently borrowed some money and the students offered to beat him for the amount he had borrowed.

The school is denying some specific parts of the abuse like the students stealing his card and is also claiming that the victim is not disabled.
