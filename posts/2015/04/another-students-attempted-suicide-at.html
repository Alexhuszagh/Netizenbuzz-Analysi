Another student's attempted suicide at Mapo Bridge saved by policewoman

Wednesday, April 22, 2015  nonentertainment, suicide  77 comments


Article: Policewoman holds the hand of a 'bullied' high school student in attempted suicide... netizens 'tear up'

Source: Herald Econ via Nate

Another female high school student was found at the Mapo Bridge with an intention to commit suicide because she was being bullied at school. A policewoman took the report and headed out to the bridge to find her. Fellow police caught the meaningful exchange between the two in a picture as she knelt to console her and help her return to her parents safe and sound.
