Brave 'Red backpack girl' helps man in drunk driving accident

Tuesday, April 21, 2015  nonentertainment  443 comments



Article: Brave 'red backpack' female student runs into a road to save bus driver

 Source: Yonhap News via Nate

A bus driver had noticed a drunk driver weaving in and out of the streets and got out of his own bus to physically block the drunk driver by standing in front of his white car to prevent any accidents. The driver pushed the man over with his car and sped away, leaving the bus driver on the middle of a busy road.

Without hesitation, a female high school student, nicknamed 'the red backpack girl' on SNS, helped the man and began shouting out the license plate number of the drunk driver for anyone watching to jot down. She then pleaded for someone to call for help while she looked after the bus driver.

The drunk driver was caught by police 600m from the scene thanks to reports.
