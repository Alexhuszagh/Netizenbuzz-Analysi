 Pann: The reality behind Leeteuk's supposed atrocities?

(Summarized translations)

(caption of Ilbe members putting together a collection of Leeteuk's 'atrocities')

I will break down what actually happened one by one.

1. BoA's cake incident

2. Kim Yuna scandal

Leeteuk apologized to Kim Yuna on 'Strong Heart' but the PD told him that it'd be funny if he said that he was rejected by her instead so Leeteuk read according to the script and the scandal blew up in the media. The PD acted like he did nothing wrong and Leeteuk took the blame for everything.

3. The April Fools' scandal

Leeteuk tweeted his phone number with the last three numbers missing. He didn't think that fans would try to guess the missing numbers since it's a 1/1000 chance that they'll get it anyway but fans started randomly guessing numbers like 1004 (his nickname) and started calling random people with the numbers.

Leeteuk realized what was going on and immediately released his entire phone number. Of course there are victims in this case but Leeteuk not only apologized but also called those numbers himself and apologized to each of them.

4. The mosquito Cyworld post

People misunderstood the post for the mosquitos being Sones. A Sone saw the post and started spreading it everywhere and it became a topic of ridicule amongst them. At the time, Elf and Sones were fighting over Dream Concert seating so Leeteuk's post naturally became something that they made fit their situation.

Leeteuk deleted the diary post after he realized people were misunderstanding it.

But the person Kyuhyun respects the most is Leeteuk. If the two really weren't that close, could Leeteuk have said all that on broadcast openly like that? And by the way, when 'U' won #1 for the first time and Kyuhyun was awkwardly standing in fear of what others thought, Leeteuk went over and hugged him. It was only then that Kyuhyun broke down and cried.

"When Super Junior got into a car accident, there was nobody around them until Jun Jin's van was passing by them because he had a schedule at the time. Jun Jin yelled back and Leeteuk kept crying and asking for him to get the other members instead of him so Jun Jin got mad at him. They all eventually got rescued by the ambulance that came.

There was a corner on 'Kiss the Radio' where the members got a chance to thank the people they were grateful for and Leeteuk started crying and said that he was so thankful to Jun Jin and his manager because he was so shocked by the situation that he didn't realize he was crying and yelling at him. Jun Jin heard the radio and later met him on broadcast and called him a fool (playful manner)."

And also, when Kyuhyun had his solo showcase, Leeteuk attended it with Changmin and Ryeowook. That day, however, was the one day off that Leeteuk gets in a year and he spent it talking to journalists at the showcase to ask them to take care of Kyuhyun.
