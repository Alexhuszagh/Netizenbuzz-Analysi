More and more Koreans enjoy being 'alone'

Monday, April 13, 2015  nonentertainment, society  144 comments


Article: [Society] More people become experts at 'ostracizing themselves'

 Source: Weekly Donga via Naver

With living alone shows like 'I Live Alone' hitting #1 in viewer ratings, many Koreans are also enjoying the solo life and aren't afraid to go out to eat, watch movies, take walks/ride bikes, spend time with pets, and enjoy sports activities alone. Surveys have also shown that the sales for 'one person' furniture have gone up 8.4%, and continue to grow by the year.
