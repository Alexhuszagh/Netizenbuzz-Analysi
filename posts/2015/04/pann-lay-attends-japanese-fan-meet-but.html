Hate on him only for the facts. The facts are that he did not attend EXO's 2nd album music show promotions in Korea and their fan meeting but did attend Japan's fan meeting.

(All screencaps in source. Keep in mind that translations are from Chinese -> Korean -> English)

Lay: But honestly speaking, when I tried to discuss all of this with the company, my family didn't believe (it could happen).

(Fan comment: So your family held you back saying it'd be impossible but you kept requesting your personal company to SM and finally became your own Chinese CEO ^^)
