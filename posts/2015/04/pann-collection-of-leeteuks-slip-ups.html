[Pann] Collection of Leeteuk's slip-ups

Thursday, April 09, 2015  leeteuk, pann, super junior  1121 comments

Pann: Leeteuk's goosebump inducing atrocities

Straight translation of everything in the post, you can find caps and stuff in the source.

-


While talking about how there's an order that the members must wait in to get opportunities for shows, the members brought up the fact that Kyuhyun landed a 'Radio Star' MC spot despite it not being his turn yet.

Kyuhyun explained that because he's the group's vocalist, he never got any variety opportunities until Kim Gura discovered his potential and suggested that he be cast on 'Radio Star' (Heechul had originally wanted Eunhyuk) but Kim Gura's suggestion won over his.

Leeteuk then said, "I don't want Kyuhyun to do variety shows. If he's not going to focus entirely on varieties, I'd rather that he not get into it at all. He's already good at singing and musicals, but if he does varieties on top of that, he'll lose his identity as well as the things he's good at."

Kyuhyun argued that the reason he does varieties is to become a better singer because sometimes singing isn't all that there is to a singer. He said he wanted to gain public recognition through the show so that people won't think some no-name member of Super Junior is singing on stage. Getting more public recognition will also give him more opportunities to sing.

Leeteuk then quoted Lee Kyung Kyu saying variety should never be a stepping stone to other opportunities. Leeteuk indirectly ended up hating on Yoon Jong Shin who's also a variety star and musician, as well as contradicting himself since he's an idol and a variety star.

-


The famous Kim Yuna scandal, which he used on variety shows by talking about it and getting Yuna hate.

But when someone else uses him and talks about him on variety shows, he tweets immediately and disses them: "Stop talking about me and stay quiet if you have no other stories to tell. Makes me realize that my image is made by those closest to me. Time to take care of myself now."

-

Indirectly dissed a junior group who won #1 on the Oricon charts in Japan by tweeting about Super Junior's own achievements in Japan and claiming they didn't use any 'petty tricks'.

Tweet: "(Copy & paste of news article detailing SJ's Japanese achievements) We didn't use any petty tricks~^^ "

-


Smashed a cake into BoA's face, which pissed BoA off and made her tweet, "These days, so many people cross the line... At least apologize right away if you're sorry.. Why run away after committing the act?" and then tweet to Leeteuk, "You crossed a line yesterday."

Then Leeteuk tweeted on his own, "I'm the easiest person for everyone to mistreat, it seems.. Fine, hate on me everyone... The only way to become the bigger person is to laugh it off.. hahahahahaha ......."

Then when BoA started getting tons of hate from SHINee fans, Leeteuk tweeted, " Aigoo, how funny... you reap what you sow.. " clearly an indirect tweet at BoA, which BoA promptly unfollowed him for her.

Then Leeteuk tweeted again, "Sob, I got caught ..." and "But now that this has happened, nothing I can do about it.. "

-


On his mini-homepage (2010), he wrote a post that instigated a fight between SNSD fans SuJu fans:

"The mosquito is sitting on the tiger's back and sucking his blood. The mosquito thinks he was victorious over the tiger... but the tiger didn't care at all. This is the difference between an expert and those beneath them. Don't react to the little things..."

And once the fandom fight got out of hand, he lied that his mini-homepage got hacked.
