Hani clarifies that she will not be retiring after contract expires

Sunday, April 19, 2015  exid, hani  146 comments


Article: Hani clarifies retirement rumors "I was thoughtless when I said that... I want to be with EXID in 10 years"

Source: No Cut News via Nate

In the past, Hani had said that she wanted to study retire from singing and study to become a psychologist after her contract expires.

A fan recently asked her again if she was going to retire and Hani replied, "I need to clarify this. I can't quit even after these promos. I'm still tied to my contract so I have a few years left (laughter). I think it's foolish to determine your life in a very moment when you don't know what's going to happen in the future or if you're going to change your mind. What I said on broadcast was me being honest about what I wanted to do in the future, but I realize that I was thoughtless with what I said and I'm sorry for worrying my fans. I promise to be EXID's Hani even 10 years from now."
