Korea's 2015 job prospect report

Tuesday, April 21, 2015  nonentertainment  257 comments

Article: Elementary/junior/high school teachers along with 32 other jobs to be cut down in 10 years

Source: Newsis via Naver

Korea's 2015 job prospect report showed the following:

Increase in 10 years: event planners, counseling specialists, safety managers, machinery technicians, app developers, business consultants

Maintain in 10 years: visual designers, communication technicians, credit/investment analysts, appraisal experts

Decrease in 10 years: elementary/junior/high school teachers, stock/foreign exchange brokers, photographers, salespeople
