Pann: It's too hard for female idols to win #1 on music shows right now..

With the difference in how male and females act, it's currently unfair for female idols and makes it impossible for them to win #1 on music shows.

Digital songs are easily downloadable and accessible by men but men rarely ever buy a physical copy... Realistically, considering the circumstances, I think EXO and miss A are both equal in popularity and both deserve a co-#1.
