Why is Suzy still the talk of the town even after her dating scandal?

Wednesday, April 08, 2015  miss a, suzy  154 comments


Article: Why are men still going crazy over Suzy 'in a relationship'?

Source: Ilgan Sports via Nate

As long as there are three or more men in a group, the topic of conversation will automatically default to Suzy whether it's at work, out at dinner, or in a group chat. With her recent comeback with miss A and the success of 'Only You', there's only been more to talk about.

"Didn't know Suzy could sing this well", "I thought she was small but she looks at least 170 cm tall", are just some of the many comments. Despite her relationship with Lee Min Ho going public, many worried over a 'comeback tragedy' but the situation is anything but and actually quite the opposite.

There are probably many reasons to this but the biggest is Suzy blossoming into her beauty. She still hadn't yet grown into her looks up to last year and still fit high school roles, which of course garnered the love of oppas, but it wasn't as strong as it is today.

But with Suzy losing her baby fat, she matured and unveiled glimpses of a sexier image behind her innocent face. As she is known to be, Suzy's a natural beauty, and in an industry that is full of duplicate faces, her face is above and beyond them all.

She's also improved in singing talents and is now considered a lead vocalist in miss A in terms of skill. And without having to be 'considered for being an idol', her acting has also stabilized. Although her image is still being bombarded everywhere, there's still a freshness to her that's maintained.

Suzy dating Lee Min Ho has also not put her oppa fans in despair. If anything, their sentiment has only gone from 'Could our Suzy date?' to 'Our Suzy is dating, she is a woman too'. It has actually benefited Suzy since she's been slowly unveiling glimpses of her sexy, mature side anyway.

With the situation as it is, many love calls are heading her way, and her public relationship hasn't impacted her deals at all. It's to the point where she has so many backed up deals that she can pick and choose from them. One insider says, "Everyone's looking for Suzy, no one else. She's actually become an even 'hotter' topic with her dating top star Lee Min Ho going public. It is a strange phenomenon but it's possible because it's Suzy. She has a beauty unlike any other and there's nothing to fault her in terms of her singing and acting career. There's no celebrity to threaten her either. It'll be a Suzy generation for a while."
