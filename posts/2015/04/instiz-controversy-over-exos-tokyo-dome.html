[Instiz] Controversy over EXO's Tokyo Dome tour

Sunday, April 19, 2015  exo, instiz, tokyo dome  638 comments




Instiz: SM looks to be in a rush

Yunho said at TVXQ's last Japanese tour concert there won't be anymore TVXQ tours for the time being with his upcoming enlistment. A financial analyst said that TVXQ bring in the most income in SM (one tour roughly brings in $100 million USD). Without TVXQ, SM loses out on $100 million in income a year. Because they need a group to fill in for the missing money, they're shipping EXO off to Japan and debuting Rookiez in Korea.

-

Now other fandoms are angry that EXO is getting a "free ride" into the Tokyo Dome without even a Japanese song.
