Kang Yewon admits to struggling with binge eating on the 1 meal 1 day diet

Wednesday, April 08, 2015  diet, kang yewon  89 comments


Article: Kang Yewon "I gained 4 kg during 'Real Men'... now on a 1 meal 1 day diet"

Source: OSEN via Naver

When asked how she manages her body, she replied, "I'm on a 1 meal 1 day diet so I binge at dinner time. If I binge at lunch, I end up eating again at night so I have to work out morning and night. I have to diet like this because if I eat all three meals a day, I end up gaining weight. That's why I gained 4 kg at the army. I love eating but I make up for it by working out."
