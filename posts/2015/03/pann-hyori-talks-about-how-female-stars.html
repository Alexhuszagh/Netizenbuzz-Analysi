[Pann] Hyori talks about how female stars are treated in the work place

Tuesday, March 10, 2015  hyori, pann  78 comments


Pann: How female celebrities are treated on set

Pann talks about how advertisers will abuse their power over celebrities, especially female celebrities, by treating them like sexual objects.

Hyori talks about a particular incident that enraged her where she was shooting a CF that required her to drink some water and the producer was like, "Hyori-ssi, how about you drip some of that into your cleavage?!"

Another incident was when an actress arrived at the film set and the producers asked for a nude scene that she was never informed of and when she refused, they were like, "You took your clothes off for tons of other movies, what's wrong with you..."
