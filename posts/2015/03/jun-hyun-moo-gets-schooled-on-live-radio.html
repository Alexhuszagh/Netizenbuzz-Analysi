Jun Hyun Moo gets schooled on live radio

Thursday, March 05, 2015  jun hyun moo  145 comments


Article: Jun Hyun Moo gets reprimanded during live radio "Don't tell kids to throw their dreams away"

Source: Mydaily via Nate

One mother who was listening to his radio show called in live with some words of wisdom: "I don't hate your broadcast but I don't like the way you put things.

You mentioned on broadcast that it's better to throw away dreams. I don't think that's right. It is your duty as a public figure to tell young kids that they need to have hopes and dreams whether they come true or not. That is your responsibility. When kids are already suffering from a gloomy outlook, you need to tell them to hold on to their dreams, not throw them away."

He said back, "I didn't mean for them to throw their dreams away but that maybe holding on to them might not be the best..."

She said, "Even if it's a dream that isn't meant to come true, they should hold on to it anyway. A dream is not solely meant to be achieved. A dream is something meant to be chased. That chase is what makes up life. How can a human being live for the sole purpose of achieving a goal? Having a dream in itself is something positive."

She also added, "And stop telling kids to get plastic surgery. You say that botox is good for this and that, but stop. It's not even an inexpensive procedure."

Jun Hyun Moo concluded, "I'll take your words to heart. Thank you for your words of advice. I'll say wise things in the future. I love you, mother."
