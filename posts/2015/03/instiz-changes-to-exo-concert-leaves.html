[Instiz] Changes to EXO concert leaves fans angry

Friday, March 13, 2015  exo, instiz  396 comments

Instiz: Fans are mad because of EXO's concert right now

SM basically changed the content of EXO's concert midway through its run and fans who attended earlier are angry that they paid the same amount for tickets but got less content.

EXO's 'The EXO'LuXion' concert was held on March 7th and 8th in the first week and on the 13th, 14th, and 15th on the second week.

All tickets on all dates cost $110. The content between the first and second weeks, however, changed.

1. Playboy was changed to Sehun and Lay and Kai danced alone
2. Runway walking with female models for 'Machine' (7th/8th had no runway X)
3. Growl dubstep + MAMA version (7th/8th had no dubstep versions X)
4. The VCR changed for 'Promise'
5. Thunder + Into Your World double encore (7th/8th had no Thunder encore X)
6. The concert was a little under 3 hours but the second week went over 3 hours
7. Outfits changed a bit and Xiumin revealed his abs under a mesh shirt (7th/8th Xiumin did not show anything at all X...)
8. As an event, threw marshmallows + autographed balls into the crowd (7th/8th no autographed balls and the second week had better marshmallow wrapping + other content with it)
