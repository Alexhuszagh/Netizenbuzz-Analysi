Couple along with 3 year old daughter commit suicide after meeting on a suicide site

Monday, March 09, 2015  nonentertainment, suicide  181 comments

Article: Couple who met on a suicide site go through with suicide 

Source: Yonhap News via Nate

A 37 year old man and a 38 year old woman who met on a suicide site two months ago went through with their suicide pact, taking with them the woman's three year old daughter, all three of whom were discovered dead in a room together with a one page will.

After initially meeting, the two had tried to get through life together again but gave up after two months. The man had been suffering from financial issues related to his cancer treatments and the woman was also suffering from financial issues from her husband's addiction to gambling. The will stated, "Sorry to our families for leaving first. We tried to live as hard as we could but it didn't work out."
