    Female empowerment and the assumption that every choice a woman makes is empowering because it's a choice she made for herself is not the same thing. It isn't that Hyosung is doing anything wrong, but she's playing herself into the role set for her by a patriarchal society as a woman-- and because Hyosung is choosing to do so, it's expected of other women to follow suit.

    So it's pretty easy to see why it can be a problem. Even though I fully support a woman doing as she pleases, I'd also hope that people stop and understand what choice feminism is and how it can be entirely counteractive to the feminist movement, and why some women may not find her choices to be particularly empowering.
