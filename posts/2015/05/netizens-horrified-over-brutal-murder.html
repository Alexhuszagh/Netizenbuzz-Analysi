Netizens horrified over brutal murder of baby cat

Saturday, May 09, 2015  nonentertainment  247 comments

Article: Netizens horrified over cruel murder of headless cat

Source: Yonhap News via Nate [google image search of the picture here, it is censored but still GRAPHIC]

A neighborhood fell into some conflict when a family began putting out cat kibble for the street cats in the area. Other neighbors were opposed to the idea as they didn't want to deal with the street cat population and as an act of warning, someone anonymous cruelly murdered a baby cat by beheading it and placing its body on top of the bowl of kibbles laid out for the cats. Someone took a picture of it and spread it on SNS.

Netizens are demanding police investigation into the issue but police are waiting for a proper police report before acting further.
