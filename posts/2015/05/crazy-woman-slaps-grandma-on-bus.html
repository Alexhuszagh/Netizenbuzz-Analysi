Crazy woman slaps grandma on the bus, stopped by 'candy girl'

Friday, May 01, 2015  nonentertainment  205 comments


Article: Bus assault in Chungju... middle aged woman slaps grandma, 'candy girl' praised for blocking the assault

 Source: Star News via Nate

Video went viral on Korean sites of a grandma who started getting assaulted just for sitting down next to a middle aged woman. The woman demanded that she change seats, which the grandma did, but then the woman followed and slapped the grandma again. A girl holding candy got up and blocked the fight from escalating until the woman was forced to get off the bus.

The grandma and her son have reported the matter to the police and the video is being used to track the woman down.
