Teacher keeps promise to see his students again in 20 years

Thursday, May 14, 2015  nonentertainment  158 comments



Article: "I'll put out a TV ad in 20 years so let's all meet then" Teacher keeps his promise to his students

Source: Korea Ilbo via Naver

A phys ed teacher made a promise to his graduating students that he'd put out a TV ad in 20 years for them to see and contact him. The promise was kept and the teacher got to meet all of his students at a restaurant where the men got on their knees and bowed before him out of respect.
