Washington Post spotlights growing trend of Korean men wearing make up

Monday, May 11, 2015  nonentertainment  158 comments


Article: Washington Post spotlights Korean men wearing make up, "Korean men in twenties focus on make up"

 Source: Yonhap News via Nate

Increase in male skincare and make up products in Korea's cosmetics industry every year. Most popular products start with basic skincare like toners, essence, and lotion up to eyebrow pencils, bb cream, and popular websites that give make up tips.

One make up brand recently passed out free cleansing and toner samples to males at a baseball game.

The trend increase is believed to be because of the importance in looks for employment opportunities.

One 48 year old man who uses face masks after coming home from outdoor activities said, "Korean society is extremely competitive. While the younger men are stacking up resume experience or hanging out with their girlfriends, taking care of our looks can also be of big help to the older male generation."

Most make up brands are currently amping up their male make up lines as well.
