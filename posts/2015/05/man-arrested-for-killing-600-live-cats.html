Man arrested for killing 600 live cats in boiling water

Wednesday, May 20, 2015  animals, nonentertainment  419 comments




Article: 600 cats slaughtered alive by being boiled in hot water

Source: Yonhap News via Naver

A man in his fifties was arrested for killing 600 live cats by throwing them in hot water. He had captured the cats near households by alluring them with chicken and other foods. After boiling them for two minutes, he skinned them, gutted them, and then sold them for ~$15 to people who supposedly believed that eating cats would help with joint problems. The cats were killed and sold over the span of a year. 18 cats were still waiting to be slaughtered when officials broke into the slaughterhouse.
