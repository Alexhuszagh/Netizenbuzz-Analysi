Elementary school kids forced to sign suicide prevention vow

Monday, May 18, 2015  nonentertainment  637 comments


Article: 1st year elementary school students asked to sign vows not to commit suicide with witness signatures

 Source: Kukmin Ilbo via Nate

'Vow to Love Life'

I will think preciously of my life and love and respect myself and promise to never commit suicide.
1. I, _____, will never commit suicide and will never attempt to harm myself or commit suicide.
2. I will take appropriate breaks and sleep for my health.
3. I will get rid of all tools that can aid in a suicide and not depend on alcohol or drugs.
4. In order to prevent suicide, I will consult with someone around me about my worries and difficulties if I'm feeling unwell.
