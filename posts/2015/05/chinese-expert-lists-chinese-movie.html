Chinese expert lists the Chinese movie industry, money, and the Korean system as reasons for Luhan and Kris' leave

Friday, May 22, 2015  exo, kris, luhan  163 comments


Article: Chinese expert says the reason for Luhan and Kris' change of heart "Movies, money, and the system"

Source: TV Report via Naver

"The Korean entertainment industry is centered around music. Chinese celebrities start by training as singers in Korea. The Chinese entertainment industry, however, is centered around the movie industry. When Chinese celebrities who have trained in Korea return to China, they first wonder how to enter the movie industry. When Korean entertainment agencies aren't able to provide a way for them to enter the movie industry, the celebrity is left feeling unstable about his future.... Korean celebrities are also paid less in comparison to Chinese celebrities. Which industry would a celebrity prefer to promote in then?

Chinese celebrities are currently setting up their own personal studios once they get their name out there. The existence of the internet allows for cheaper promotion options, and the relationship between the celebrity and the agency has also weakened. Large Korean agencies, however, try to control the celebrity."

The expert suggested that in order to prevent Chinese members from leaving the groups/companies, that Korean companies give them more freedom, train them in the Chinese market, and work in cooperation with more Chinese agencies to manage their artists.
