    The info about the auditions on Sunday/Monday came from people who were there all day Sunday (through Monday morning for one). They checked in with me fairly regularly throughout since they couldn't leave and there wasn't much else to do. They mentioned to me first that they saw famous rappers getting pre-assigned numbers and audition times, I didn't ask about it. One said he saw a lot of famous rappers (idols and underground) who all were taken away to either audition or to come back at a later set time after being in line for a couple hours and filming a little bit.


    I'm not saying that all the famous rappers do it, but I did ask around after that, and it's pretty standard practice for famous rappers to have pre-assigned audition times that they know about beforehand. They go a bit early to film them in line, and then either leave because they have work to do elsewhere, or to eat or rest, and come back later for their set time, or go directly in to audition. More than that, most famous rappers (idol and underground, btw) sign contracts with Mnet before the auditions that set how much screen time they get, guidelines on how they can get cut, and so on. They attend meetings with the show writers to plan their story lines, and film back story in the month before auditions that will air in the episodes leading up to the final 16.


    So, there is a fair amount of special privileges handed out to famous contestants or those with people backing them who have negotiating power. I'm sure not all of them take part, as there is the rare famous rapper who signs up on their own without telling anyone. But, just know that a fair amount of them do take advantage of these perks, though it's often at their managements' behest.
