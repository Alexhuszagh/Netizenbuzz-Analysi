[Pann] 'Sober' vs 'Glad You Came' break down

Wednesday, July 01, 2015  big bang, glad you came, pann, sober  475 comments

Article: Big Bang 'Sober' plagiarized

The Wanted - Glad You Came, the songs are totally the same!!

1. [+180, -35] According to another user: it's not plagiarized at all. I saw the suspicions so I took a listen for myself and 'Glad You Came' has a B♭ cord with a main E♭ - Gm. 'Sober', on the other hand, is on a C key with the main cord being F - C - Am - G. The keys of the two songs are different and the part that you're all thinking sounds the same sound completely different when you match the keys up. If you change the key of 'Glad You Came' to the same C key as 'Sober', the melody compares like this: 'Glad You Came' lalala, lasol, mimimi, redo (repeat). 'Sober' lalalasol, mimimimire, mimimipa mi. The song was definitely not plagiarized.

2. [+180, -22] What's plagiarized about it? Why is it that whenever a new song is released, it always gets accused of plagiarism.

6. [+116, -11] I don't know... the beat sounds different

7. [+72, -7] Doesn't seem fair to accuse plagiarism when you're not even sure yet, that's how rumors start.

8. [+57, -5] This was discussed on another site and people already decided it wasn't plagiarized. There's only 10 seconds where it sounds similar but the cord and melody break downs are different. Even the songs on Melon now... 7 out of 10 of them sound like you've heard them somewhere before but you don't call that plagiarism. It's like people want Big Bang to have plagiarized so they can blame them.
