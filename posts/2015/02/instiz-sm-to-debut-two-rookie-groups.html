[Instiz] SM to debut two rookie groups this year?

Tuesday, February 24, 2015  instiz, sm entertainment  287 comments

Instiz: "SM to debut at minimum two rookie groups this year..." - KB Investments

On the 24th, KB Investments released a statement saying that SM will be debuting two new rookie groups at minimum this year which will be expected to be reflected in their stock price.

Researcher Kim Min Jung had to say, "...Since the second half of last year, SM's stock momentum has weakened with a mere 2.5% growth compared to the previous year. Their lackluster records have investors nervous, especially with their rookie group recording comparatively slight records against competing companies, and the focal talking point seems to be the army enlistment of their male idol groups and the need for a new rookie artist to make up for the promotion schedule of TVXQ once they're away."

The researcher went on to predict that SM will be releasing two new rookie idol groups to revamp their rookie line-up and to make up for the hiatus of their current artists and their promotion restrictions (from army enlistments and whatnot)."
