Jinnabit no longer decided to stay quiet about all of this and has been replying to questions and preparing lawsuits against all of the false rumors/accusations.

The latest update is that Sulli, scared of the consequences, apparently passed her number to SM to deal with, causing Jinnabit to get even angrier.
