Suzy's cellphone case sells out for a good cause

Tuesday, January 20, 2015  miss a, suzy  554 comments



Article: Suzy's phone case 'sold out'... donated to comfort women organization

Source: Newsen via Nate

Suzy was spotted a couple days ago using a cellphone case that was designed by a comfort woman who passed away in 2010. All proceeds made from the phone case sales are used for the grandmothers still alive today. After Suzy was seen with one, all available copies sold out.
