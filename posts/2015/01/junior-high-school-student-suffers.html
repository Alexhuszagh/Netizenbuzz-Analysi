Thursday, January 29, 2015    478 comments


Article: 'Bizarre torture to sexual harassment'... junior high school student abused at school for a year

Source: Yonhap News via Nate

Mother of a third year junior high school student put up a Daum Agora petition to spread her son's plight.

The abuse he suffered:

- Stabbed his hand with knives
- Scribbled and poked all over his body with pens
- Force fed him mixtures of toothpaste, orange peels, and dirt with snow
- Locked him in a bathroom and sprayed him with water
- Slapped him
- Forced him to lay on the floor and have the class sit on top of him, calling it a 'hamburger'
- Held him behind the classroom's curtains and forced him to pull down his pants, then took turns ripping out his pubic hair
