[Pann] god fans attempt to contact Cube to no avail for BtoB's official color

Friday, January 23, 2015  btob, god, pann  212 comments

Summary of what's generally been going around: god fans found out that BtoB's official color is sky blue and contacted Cube multiple times to change it because god's official color has been sky blue since debut.

Despite multiple efforts to contact them, Cube ignored their calls and efforts entirely. Looking for a more amicable way to get their attention, god fans started sending boxes full of office supplies like teas and toilet paper with their request to have BtoB's color changed written on them.

Cube refused to accept the packages, forcing the mail delivery men to leave them stacked outside the building. god fans made sure that the boxes did not get in the way of the entrance but later found Cube employees among others actually opening them up and scattering them around to make the place look 'trashed'.

Upset BtoB fans then started spreading rumors around Pann and SNS that god fans were 'terrorizing' the Cube building by trashing the entrance, which god fans clarified that it was not the case and that they had CCTV of others opening up the boxes and trashing the area on video and that they would release it if pressed further.

More faxes and calls that were unanswered


As a last resort, god fans then sent boxes of coffee, tea, lotion, and tissue paper to the Cube office with their request written in letters. 


Cube refused the deliveries so they were stacked outside in an orderly fashion



Then randomly the boxes were opened and the contents were trashed




and BtoB fans began blaming fan god for the 'trashing' to tarnish their image, leading god fans to ask back how they had any right to open packages that weren't delivered to their name and how they took pictures of the contents when it did not even belong to them. Fan god claims to have CCTV of others opening the packages and trashing the area and will release it if necessary.
