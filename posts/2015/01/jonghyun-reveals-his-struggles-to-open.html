Jonghyun reveals his struggles to open up and show his true self

Tuesday, January 13, 2015  jonghyun, shinee  68 comments


Article: Jonghyun tears up, "I feel like people judge me without knowing me"

Source: TV Report via Naver

"I don't think I've had many people that I could open up to about my feelings like this. I was always worried about what people would think of me if I talked about these things with them. No matter what I say, people have always judged me however they thought of me. It made me regretful... I used to have this greed to want to show everyone who I really am. But now, I've realized that it's impossible and that I should instead try to understand why those people are like that. I said so before... people aren't curious about what kind of person I really am."
