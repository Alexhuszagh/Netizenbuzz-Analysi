Pann: The first time Yura got mad at Hong Jong Hyun

Pann talks about a scene in a recent episode where Yura was trying to joke around with Hong Jong Hyun by pretending to brush dirt off on him but he wasn't reciprocating and just brushing her off, hurting Yura's feelings. Then he uses one of the three coupons Yura gave him to ask her to put on a fashion show in the middle of the supermarket so he can get a quick laugh, hurting her feelings even further for using her special coupons like that. Eric sympathizes with her, saying he wants to give her a hug because it's unfair how Hong Jong Hyun is being cold to her.
