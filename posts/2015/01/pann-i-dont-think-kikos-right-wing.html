Wednesday, January 21, 2015  mizuhara kiko, pann  214 comments


Pann: I don't think Mizuhara Kiko is a right-wing (My opinions)

I'm not a GD fan and never knew much about Kiko but I kept seeing her get hate for being a right-wing so I looked up some stuff.

So people call her a right-wing but I don't think so, at least from a few things I've found, like a picture of the Korean flag she uploaded on her Instagram after her scandal with GD came out. Of course it could be for image management but a right-wing would never do something like that.


She also wrote an Instagram post in Korean when she attended the Sohu Awards.

I honestly think she just lacks common knowledge since she's been living on her own since junior high school.

In this video http://youtu.be/LZhj2t2MyPQ, she talks about her mom being Korean. Why would a right-wing mention that? A real right-wing would hide that kind of information.


And a lot of people are accusing her of liking a picture of someone throwing up the middle finger at the Kyungbuk palace but it's actually a picture of the Tiananmen Square... I mean that's still stupid of her to like that picture but at least she apologized for it.

She just seems really foolish and doesn't think her actions through but it doesn't necessarily make her a right-wing.
