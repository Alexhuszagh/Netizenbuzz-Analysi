Sunday, January 04, 2015  infinite, pann  271 comments

Pann: Disney is sensitive to copyright

I'm sure you all know how sensitive Disney is to copyright issues. Remember that one instance in Japan where a bunch of little kids drew Mickey Mouse at their playground and Disney demanded that they erase it so they did?

Anyway, last summer, a video started circulating among Inspirits of a song titled 'Queen of the Dance Floor'. It's a song released by Disney and if you take a listen, the song is, from start to finish, an entire copy of INFINITE's debut track 'Come Back Again' with the lyrics changed around.

Take a listen to this video: http://youtu.be/ITEp7eN5PB4
and if you have earphones, this has dual audio: http://youtu.be/LnHZMUzQNF8

It's the same, isn't it? And when all of this was happening, only one article on it was ever released.

What fans are frustrated about right now is that there hasn't been any new article on its update.
