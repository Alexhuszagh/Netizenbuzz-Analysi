INFINITE suffers for Golden Disk's lack of preparation

Friday, January 16, 2015  29th golden disk awards, infinite  193 comments


Article: Why did the Golden Disk bother inviting INFINITE?

Source: Kuki News via Nate

Article talks about how the Golden Disk never provided INFINITE with transportation to get from the airport to the concert hall. They had to ride the subway surrounded by staff and body guards to keep the fans at bay. Woollim reps replied that they still haven't been given a proper explanation from Golden Disk as to why they weren't provided transportation.

Moreover, ceremonies usually also provide catering food or lunch boxes but INFINITE had to eat cup ramen in their waiting room to quickly fill their hunger.

INFINITE also was scheduled to perform three stages but the lights were cut off during their last stage. Golden Disk did not give an explanation and just cut to the next team scheduled.
