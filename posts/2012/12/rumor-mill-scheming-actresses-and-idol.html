[Rumor Mill] Scheming actresses and idol groups at risk of disbandment

Monday, December 24, 2012  entertainment radar, off the record, rumor mill  125 comments


1. Famous actress 'A' is said to be hitting on rich married men left and right. Her brand name bags and the apartment she's living in now are all said to have been gifts from these men. Lately, insiders have noticed that she's been trying hard to look like she's living the life of the wealthy, but those around her are supposedly sick of this hypocritical behavior. She was once sued by the wife of one of the men but the man canceled the lawsuit, proving just how charming she must be to them...

2. Another top actress 'B' is said to use any man she can if it's for the success of her career. She always leaves herself open to advances from any powerful men. She recently broke up with 'C' who had stuck with her through her long rookie period once once of her projects hit success but is now trying to get back with him after finding out that 'C' is doing just as well.

3. Actor 'B' is the hot topic of TV drama officials for his arrogant attitude. The young star has grown to becoming the lead roles after the successes of several dramas. The growth of his love calls unfortunately has also meant the growth of his arrogance. He's also taken to ignoring the broadcast companies and PDs that have made him who he is today. One official said, "Before his popularity, he would treat everyone well and everyone used to talk about how respectful he was. Seemingly overnight, he's changed so much that it's put everyone in utter shock. If this is who his true self really is, he's a great actor off screen as he is onscreen."

4. Rumors are spreading around the industry that there is some discord within a popular idol group. A couple of the members have expressed their discontentment to the company and are considering leaving the group, putting the group at risk for disbandment. The reason for these problems is none other than money. The company is said to be failing at properly distributing their income, causing a few of the members to express their frustrations outright. Insiders that came across the rumors had to say, "Such problems always arise once a group starts to do well" and "Handling money problems properly is one of the most important aspects to ensuring longevity of your group's career."

5. Another band that swept the year by storm is also facing a crisis, but this time for the imbalanced treatment of the members. Only one of the members has been enjoying high popularity with frequent TV appearances and CF love calls, causing a gaping difference in the income distribution with the other members. This band is known for their free-spirited attitudes by their own company and broadcast companies so they don't expect any huge changes in the foreseeable future. One insider had to say, "The problem hasn't reached a point where it'd affect their loyalty to one another, but with one member steadily starting to make sure money is being put in only his wallet, it seems their promotional activities might be affected. The band's future is likely at stake with how they handle this matter."

-

Sources: Off the Record, Entertainment Radar
