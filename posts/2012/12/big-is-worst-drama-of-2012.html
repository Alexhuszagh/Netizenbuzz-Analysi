Sunday, December 16, 2012  big, korean drama, korean dramas  113 comments


Article: Worst 5 drama moments in 2012

Source: Mediaus via Nate

Editorial that ranked the top five worst moments in the 2012 dramas. #5 was a scene in "Can't Live Without You" where 'Seo Ga Young' was threatening a young child to open the door. #4 was in "Oohlala Couple" when 'Na Yeo Ok' had a forced abortion after returning to her body. #3 was the open ending in "Big". #2 was the shocking ending in "Fashion King". #1 was the embarrassing scene in "Big" when 'Gil Da Ran' claims "I love KKJ" at a restaurant in front of his parents.

3. [+376, -29] Big was really trash

5. [+54, -12] I was really surprised at the Hong sisters' ability to take Lee Min Jung and Gong Yoo and create such trash. The synopsis sounded fun in itself but the actual process of it playing out had no point whatsoever.
