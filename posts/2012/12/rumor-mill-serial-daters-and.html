[Rumor Mill] Serial daters and celebrities with double lives

Monday, December 24, 2012  entertainment radar, rumor mill  60 comments


6. Popular female soloist 'A' has begun dating a male idol group member after overcoming a difficult break up. 'A' had previously dated another popular idol group member before the break up. The new idol that stole her heart is said to be similar in style to the previous idol in that they're both cute, pretty boy types.

7. Actress 'B' best known for her innocent image is said to be a serial octopus dater. 'B' has a tendency to date a lot of men at once from Hallyu stars to idol stars using her sweet, innocent looks to her advantage. 'B' was most recently said to have been in a relationship with a man quite older than her.

8. Producers were recently surprised with the extent of support a company is willing to give for top star 'C'. 'C' recently guested on a popular variety program that called for two to three personal staff members at most, but she ended up showing up six - way too many for the producers to accommodate. To make matters worse, the staff would follow her wherever she moved, making it difficult for cameras to follow 'C', let alone catch her on camera. Producers said that although the show has seen many top stars in its days, C's case is definitely unique.

9. Lately, there have been so much news about male celebrities having a secret personal life with wives and children that fans and the public never knew about. This has caused quite a stir in the industry, with many probing managers for details on how many more such male celebrities there are. One actor 'D' is said to have such a rumor following him as well. 'D' had previously been involved in gay rumors due to his close friendship with another Hallyu star, but according to recent rumors, he's shacked up with a longtime girlfriend, making him the next candidate to come forward with news of a secret family.
