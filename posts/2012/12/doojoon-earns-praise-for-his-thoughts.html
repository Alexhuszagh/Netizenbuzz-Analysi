Doojoon earns praise for his thoughts on 'Happy Together'

Friday, December 14, 2012  b2st, doojoon  35 comments


Article: 'Happy Together' Yoon Doojoon's take on dating shows respect and the concerns of idols

Source: Sports Chosun via Nate

Editorial spotlighting Doojoon for the attention he's been receiving for his guesting on 'Happy Together'. He earned a lot of points for his manners and respectful actions, like refusing to call any female idols or share their numbers on the show because he wanted to respect their privacies and how he wouldn't go public with his relationship if he were dating out of respect for her because his life is already so exposed as an idol.

1. [+175, -13] I saw Happy Together last night because I heard Kim Min Jung would be on it. All this time, I thought Yoon Doojoon was just a handsome gorilla, but watching him on the show made me see that he's very respectful and reacts well to everything his seniors say. I think he's an okay idol.

2. [+151, -30] A celebrity is a human being so of course they can date, but these people wouldn't exist if not for their fans. The reason Seo Taiji kept his marriage a secret was because of his fans. It's the fans that are buying every new album, running streams, and doing support events. It's wrong to go public with your relationship when you think of that;; Fans are people and it's hard to act like it's okay when their favorite artist is dating someone else. Still, I like Yoon Doojoon's view on this. At first glance, he looks 'just nice' but if you look closer, he's actually very handsome.

3. [+79, -6] I've always liked Doojoon ever since he was called 'boyfriend idol' and 'athletic idol'. Seeing him on the show yesterday showed that he works hard on his acting and shows respect to his seniors. He seems like a good guy~
