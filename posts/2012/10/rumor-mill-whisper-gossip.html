[Rumor Mill] Whisper Gossip

Wednesday, October 24, 2012  rumor mill, whisper gossip  56 comments


1. Girl group A's member B was recently reported to have ran out of a broadcast hall in tears for her Pinocchio nose. A recently made their comeback with a new album and is in the middle of busy promotions. Like any other day, the girls were waiting for their turn on a public broadcast music program when the show's PD went over to them and denied them on the show. The reason was that B's nose was swollen as she recently had some plastic surgery done on it. Staff felt that it was too swollen to the point where they couldn't let her on the show so the PD stepped up and said they couldn't perform. The girl group eventually ran out of the hall crying.

2. Actor C who recently went through a tough break up is rumored to be dating a blonde French woman. C was recently in the headlines for breaking up with another fellow actress, but it seems he's already found a new love while at the set of his movie filming overseas. Nobody knows whether he was cheating on his ex with the new lady or if he met her afterwards, but according to insiders, he's still flying to the country his new lady is in even after filming has already been completed. If you see any articles about actor C flying to Europe next week, you'll know who it is!
