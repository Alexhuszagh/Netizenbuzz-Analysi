[Rumor Mill] Actors Edition

Saturday, October 20, 2012  rumor mill  123 comments



I will be throwing in all of the other actors/actresses as per request.

-

1. Top star 'D' who recently made a comeback after a long hiatus confessed that rookie actress 'E' whom he dated on his hiatus "wouldn't leave him alone with the skinship". It's been a year since they've been dating and 'E' never once let go of him, using his knees as a chair and his back as a backrest. Although he said he fell in love with her for her innocent ways, he's a bit tired of her now that he has to basically piggy back her around. They recently broke up.

2. Top star 'C' and her disrespectful attitude is the talk of the industry. She's famous for her great body and fashion sense and is the #1 most requested celebrity for photoshoots and fashion magazines. Unfortunately, she won't keep any of the promises she makes. At a photoshoot not too long ago, she disappeared without saying a word to the staff, making them wait for six straight hours. She called them saying that she wasn't in great condition and that she couldn't work for them anymore. She quit the job just like that despite already having received money for it.

3. Actress 'D' recently made a brave wardrobe decision for a red carpet event where she exposed a bit more skin than is normal. Her dress made headlines on portal site search rankings and was a topic of much interest. Her company was happy to have achieved the desired results for their rookie actress, but was dismayed to see that the spotlight didn't last long, as she was forgotten just a few days after the event.

4. Recently, middle aged actor Park Geun Hyung mentioned that there were a lot of undeserving actors riding on the Hallyu wave, and actor 'A' is now the topic of much discussion due to his quick rise to fame. With just a few dramas, he managed to earn top star status, but his attitude is unfortunately anything but. He'll complain about every little thing on the set and demand that his schedule be prioritized, leaving producers with a film schedule that isn't workable. One staff even quit on the spot, refusing to work with A any further. An insider said, "Unlike his outer appearance, he's one of the few actors where his attitude is different from his image. We're worried how much worse he'll get if he's already this bad at his young age. Nobody can control this guy."

5. Actor 'B' who's not that outstanding in his looks or acting skills has been getting a lot of casting offers, and it's been revealed to be through the support of an older, richer celebrity 'C'. Nobody knows why 'C' is so fond of 'B' but she'll even give him allowance money said to be in the thousands. An insider said, "Even top stars don't have that much cash in their wallets. I think I know why B is more focused on tending to C's needs instead of working out his acting controversies."

6. The industry is in a bit of a confused shock after producers of an upcoming drama next year cast a particular actress for the leading role. Originally, D and E were on the table but F managed to land the role when her name wasn't even included in the discussions. F is famous for using her looks to getting her away with producers and writers and changing the script to suit her needs. A drama staff said, "F is infamous for the way she goes through men, so there are some saying that she probably used those skills to get the lead drama role for herself."
