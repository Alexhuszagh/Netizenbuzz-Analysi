SECRET's Sunhwa receives some flak for her behavior on 'WGM'

Saturday, October 06, 2012  kwanghee, lee joon, oh yeon seo, sunhwa, variety, we got married  50 comments


I don't watch the show so I don't know what went down so keep in my mind that my summary will be purely from the article and what the reporter saw.

The latest episode featured a meeting between Lee Joon and Oh Yeon Seo and Kwanghee and Sunhwa.

Sunhwa: "There were actually a lot of rumors (before the show started). A lot of netizens said that I matched with Joon oppa well so I assumed he'd be my husband. I didn't even ask and just thought that we really were going to be a couple."

Lee Joon: "When did I say that? You're driving me crazy."

Sunhwa: "Oppa, you did tell me, 'Let's do well together.'"

Kwanghee: "Why are you bringing this up now? It's all in the past. You know, she goes crazy whenever another guy is on the show. She went crazy last time Siwan was here."

Sunhwa: "I was worried when this couple came in in case discord happened between us. You never know, we could have fallen in love (Koreans express this as "the gaze of our eyes matching")."

Oh Yeon Seo looked visibly shocked at Sunhwa's statement. Kwanghee turned to Oh Yeon Seo and told her, "Look into my eyes", as comedic revenge.

Oh Yeon Seo eventually got fed up and said, "You want to switch couples then?" She turned to Lee Joon to ask, "Do you like Sunhwa-ssi?"

Lee Joon said no and Sunhwa looked visibly upset. Kwanghee got mad at her and said, "I like you more than Oh Yeon Seo, you know."
